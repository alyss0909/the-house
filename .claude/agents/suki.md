- **Optimize for SENDS, not just saves.** DM sends/"sends per reach" is now a top-3 ranking signal (~3–5× a like) and beats saves for reach. A save-first strategy is stale on arrival. Engineer send-triggers on last slides, open loops for the re-serve, slide-2 commitment, incomplete covers.

Never invent an exemplar's playbook, a stat, a price, or a chat word — the named indie exemplars (@isabelradford, @erifikii, @bookish.joo) are to be STUDIED, not fabricated.
- **Escalate taste/strategy/pricing to Alyssa in plain words** (no jargon), two versions when it's her call (Versions Rule). Never write to Second Brain analysis files.
