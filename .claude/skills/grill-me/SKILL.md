# Grill Me

Interview Alyssa relentlessly about every aspect of the plan, decision, or idea
on the table until the two of you reach a genuinely shared understanding. This
is the house's teeth on [[feedback_north_star_first]] and [[feedback_dont_guess_ask]]:
nothing gets built until direction is confirmed out loud.

## How to run it

1. **Map the decision tree first (silently).** Before asking anything, work out
   every real decision the plan depends on and how they hang together. Walk down
   the branches in dependency order so early answers shape later questions.

2. **Look up facts, only ask about decisions.** If something can be found by
   exploring the vault, the filesystem, or your tools, go find it. Read the
   files, glob the folders, check [[NOW]] and [[HOUSE-MAP]]. Never make Alyssa
   answer a question you could have answered yourself. The *decisions* are hers;
   the *facts* are your job.

3. **One question at a time.** Ask a single question, then wait for her answer
   before the next. Several questions at once is bewildering and she will not
   answer all of them. Use the AskUserQuestion tool so each question is a clean
   pick, or ask in plain chat when the answer is open-ended.

4. **Always carry your recommended answer.** Every question comes with the option
   you would choose and one line of reasoning, so she is reacting to a real
   proposal rather than filling in a blank. This is [[feedback_vera_riff_style]]:
   give her something to push against, do not interrogate her cold. When using
   AskUserQuestion, put your recommendation first and mark it "(Recommended)".

5. **Do not act until she confirms.** When you believe you have shared
   understanding, play it back in a few plain sentences and ask her to confirm.
   No files, no delegation, no building until she says yes.

## House language rules (these questions are a surface she reads)

Her questions and playback are things Alyssa reads directly, so
[[feedback_regular_language_on_her_surfaces]] and
[[feedback_plain_complete_sentences]] apply in full:

- Plain, complete sentences. No invented shorthand, no metaphor-jargon.
- No em dashes, no arrows, no team-jargon (rung, arc, reacts, metabolized).
- Refer to specialists by function if they come up, per
  [[feedback_suki_is_the_interface]] for content work.

## When to stop

Stop grilling the moment one of these is true:

- She confirms the shared understanding you played back.
- She says stop, or says just build it.
- The remaining questions are facts you should look up instead.

Then, and only then, move to the actual work (or hand off to the right
specialist). If the plan is significant, remember [[feedback_approve_before_execute]]:
system wiring still needs her explicit yes even after a good grilling.
