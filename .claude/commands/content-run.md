# /content-run — approved options become finished posts

One writer context per post, everything loaded, no relay. This command replaces the old concept-then-hook-then-arc-then-draft ladder (retired 2026-07-16; see `Studio/Content/PIPELINE-LADDER.md` supersession note).

## Boot (light — content sessions skip house ceremony)

Load, in full, ONLY:
1. `Studio/Content/METHOD.md` — the craft authority. The deliverable format in §4 is the contract.
2. `Studio/Analysis/carousel-hook-forensics.md` — hook shapes + cover gate.
3. `Studio/Content/taste-ledger.md` — in full; Positive signal section outranks everything but her live words.
4. `Studio/Hermes/BUSINESS.md` + `Studio/Hermes/FUNNEL.md` — offers, prices, chat words, lanes.
5. **LOOK:** view the real slide images (Read tool, in order) of the TWO nearest exemplar posts from `Library/Examples/Carousels/` — one from the same lane as the post being written, one from a different lane. Text analysis is never a substitute for looking.

## Step 1 — Harvest her signal FIRST

Read the Content Studio board (Notion database ID in `/saves-sweep`'s Cache section). Before writing anything:
- Any option whose status she changed (tapped to Approved, killed, or edited) since the last run: log it verbatim in `Studio/Content/taste-ledger.md` — approvals and edits under **Positive signal**, kills under Reacts with her words if she left any.
- Any shipped post with new real numbers available (via the Composio Instagram connection, when bound): append reach, saves, comments, and ManyChat triggers to the ledger's **Shipped results** section. Real numbers are the Gate's ammo.

## Step 2 — Build the run list

The run list = every board row with Status "Approved" plus any slot Alyssa named in chat. For each entry, pull the offer's full sources: the offer's `Library/Offers/*/MAP.md`, its sales page and deliverable from `Library/Examples/`, and at least one more real Alyssa artifact (a real email, a program transcript, a journal entry on the topic). **Minimum three real sources loaded in full per post, at least one of them verbatim her writing.** A post that would rest on one MAP file gets more digging before drafting.

## Step 3 — Write (one fresh context per post, volume by default)

Per METHOD.md §4, produce for each concept:
- **Two structurally different versions** (different bit, different arc architecture — e.g. one trend-report frame, one story/POV frame). Not the same post reworded.
- Each version: 2 cover variants, every slide in her slide grammar (eyebrow/headline/subhead/body with bolds/parenthetical/footer/tactic line), functional design slots only, caption, 5 hooks (≤10 words, named shapes), CTA with the verbatim chat word or "manual selection needed", sources list.
- Each version runs at least one named mechanic from METHOD §2, declared in its tactic lines.

If dispatching writers as subagents, one post per agent, each briefed with the full boot list above plus the post's sources. Never split one post across agents.

## Step 4 — The two gates, once, at the end

Per METHOD §7: the lineup test (draft beside two real pieces of hers) and the stranger test (name the bit in one sentence). One rebuild maximum on a failure, then ship to her anyway with the concern noted. The gates filter; Alyssa decides.

## Step 5 — Deliver

Push every version to the Content Studio board: Status = "Ready for Alyssa", full copy in the page body in the slide grammar, hooks listed at top so she can gut-react fast. Report to Alyssa: how many posts, one line per post naming its bit, and any flags (missing chat word, number needing a live check). Say "ready for your picks," never "final."

## Step 6 — Close the loop

Her subsequent picks and edits on the board are harvested at the top of the NEXT run (Step 1). Nothing else to do; the loop is the learning.

## Hard rules carried forward

- Taste ledger anti-patterns are law. Her reactions get logged verbatim, typos included, same session they happen.
- Never invent product facts, prices, links, or chat words. Notion Products DB is canonical for those at ship time.
- No re-skins of published posts (`Library/Examples/Carousels/INDEX.md` is the check) and no artifact reuse (METHOD §5).
- Never CTA to Unignorable Subject Lines. Slowly Viral has no live CTA until she says so.
- FTG posts run METHOD §8, not the carousel rules.
