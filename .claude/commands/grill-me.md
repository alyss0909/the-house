# Grill Me

Thin wrapper for the [[grill-me]] skill at `.claude/skills/grill-me/SKILL.md`.
Follow that file end to end.

Usage:

```
/grill-me [what you want grilled]
```

Larry interviews you one question at a time, each with his recommended answer,
looking up facts himself and only putting real decisions to you. Nothing gets
built until you confirm shared understanding. This is the house's enforcement of
[[feedback_north_star_first]].
