# How "comments" got printed as your north star

While mocking up the HOME dashboard (2026-07-10), I put **"comments → conversations → your list → sales"** at the top of the front desk as *your north star*. You flagged it: that may be a carousel's goal, but it is not your north star. You're right, and the error is structural, not a typo. Here's the trace.

## The chain

1. **2026-07-08, Suki activation session** ([[2026-07-08_larry_suki-activation-playbook-and-hiring-plan]]): you locked comments as the north star **of the Content OS** — "the Content OS is built backwards from one useful keyword comment: comment → ManyChat DM → asset/coupon → list/sale." Correctly scoped in [[GL-012-content-os-strategy-lead-charter]] and [[Studio/Team/Suki - Social Media Strategist/PLAYBOOK|Suki's PLAYBOOK]] as a *content-strategy* charter.
2. **Same session**, I saved it to my persistent memory as "Content north star = comments." The memory itself was scoped to content.
3. **2026-07-10, HOME design session**: designing a *whole-life* dashboard, I searched what I knew for "north star," found only the content one, and **promoted it up a level without noticing the scope boundary**. A lane metric became the banner over your entire house.

## The real structural gap

The scope escalation was possible because **the house has no house-level mission written anywhere.** Nothing in Notebook, Studio, or Library states what Alyssa's actual north star is. Your Journal has personal frames ([[2023-11-21-nourishing-north-star|Nourishing North Star]] — "who is this easy for / what can I eliminate"), and Life/ has goals, but no single blessed statement exists. In a vacuum, the loudest *scoped* goal filled the frame — exactly the failure mode a single-source-of-truth house is supposed to prevent.

## What I've already fixed

- My memory now carries a hard scope guard: the comments funnel is Content OS-only and must never be presented as Alyssa's overall north star; and more generally, **a goal saved at one scope is never promoted to a wider scope without her.**
- The HOME mockup no longer claims a north star; the funnel line is labeled as the content lane's weekly job.

## The ruling this needs from you

The HOME dashboard has a masthead spot reserved for your actual north star — the thing over the whole house, above content, above any one offer. Options: you dictate it in a sentence or two (I file it verbatim, likely `Library/me.md` or a `Notebook` note you own); or we do a short riff session and you approve the wording; or the spot stays empty until it's earned. Your call.
