# Monthly Repeating Planning Grid

This is Alyssa's repeatable monthly social rhythm. It is not a March-only plan.

Theme and focus mean the same thing in this system.

## Weekly slots

| Day | Slot | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Monday | Edu Carousel + Freebie | Freebie-led education. Teach one useful thing and route to the matching freebie. |
| Wednesday | Offer | Offer-led education. Teach or reframe toward a paid product, low-ticket offer, or BOH. |
| Friday | For The Girls | Identity, relatable pattern recognition, permission, taste-world, or shareable brand-world content. |

Core rule: Wednesday offer carousels are still educational and high-value. Offer-led does not mean flat sales content.

## Monthly focus rhythm

| Week | Focus / Theme | Monday | Wednesday | Friday |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Week 1 | Email | Bingeable Newsletters; Email List Revival Plan; Soft Sundays | List Resuscitator; The 5 Minute Newsletter; A Year of Emails; Unignorable Subject Lines | Cups Labeled / Wasting Time |

| Week 4 | Productivity | Soft CEO Sessions | Soft CEO Monthly; CEO Year; 90d Clock In | Snail Girl Days / DITL vibes |

## For The Girls rule

For The Girls posts are not throwaway personality content.

They carry:

- Identity.
- Permission.
- Taste-world.
- Relatable pattern recognition.
- Shareability.
- Brand affinity.

They may have no direct CTA or a very soft CTA. That does not make them strategically weaker.
