## What this means

Her strongest fit is not "own operations." Her strongest fit is "build or maintain small systems that reduce manual work."

She should be given tasks with:
- a clear input source
- a clear tracker
- a repeatable workflow
- an obvious done state
- a simple quality check

She should not yet own:
- offer strategy
- final copy
- client-success judgment
- broad launch management
- marketing analysis without a template
- project prioritization
- ambiguous "keep things moving" work

The best first test is the podcast pitching opportunity system.

- It uses her research, CRM, follow-up, and automation skills.

## Suggested first assignment

"Camila, your first recurring project is to build and maintain a podcast opportunity tracker for Alyssa. Larry/Pax will provide criteria and AI-generated fit scoring. Wren will provide pitch templates. ## Output rhythm

### Daily

- 3 to 5 new verified opportunities added or updated
- follow-ups due today completed or flagged
- tracker status clean
- any strong-fit opportunity marked for Alyssa

### Weekly

- 10 to 15 qualified opportunities researched
- 5 to 10 pitches prepared or sent
- all follow-up dates scheduled
- short report: best opportunities, pitches sent, replies, blockers

### Monthly

- 40 to 60 opportunities reviewed
- 20 to 30 qualified targets in tracker
- 15 to 25 pitches prepared or sent
- response rate and booked/next-step outcomes summarized
- recommendations for improving target criteria and pitch angles

## Quality bar

Camila's work is successful when Alyssa can open the tracker and immediately see:

- who the opportunity is
- why it fits
- what angle to pitch
- where to contact them
- what happened last
- what happens next
- what decision, if any, Alyssa needs to make
