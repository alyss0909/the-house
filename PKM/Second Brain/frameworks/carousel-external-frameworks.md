## Framework 1 — "The Engaging Carousel Formula" (source: screenshot)

**SLIDE 1: Hook Slide (Scroll Stopper)**
- A STRONG title filled with keywords for SEO
- An engaging visual (like a video element)
- An overall clean and easy to read aesthetic

**SLIDE 2: Immediate Information**
- Instagram will often show the SECOND slide first so make sure you're getting DIRECTLY into the "meat" or the "value" of your post
- An engaging visual
- Story-telling elements

**SLIDES 3–5: Value-Drop**
- Each slide should include valuable context
- Mini takeaways
- Things like: Lists, Tips, Steps, Dos/Don'ts
- Each slide = ONE point / ONE takeaway

**LAST SLIDE: Engagement Hook**
- A recap of "aha" moment
- A highly share-able quote
- A funny anecdote or meme
- A strong and engaging CTA
