## The Pep Talk

🫶 _the weekly pep talk you didn't know you needed (from your business big sis)_ 🫶

**_I cried into my fridge this week, [FIRST NAME GOES HERE]._**

I MIGHT have been in my luteal phase… but i swear that had nothing to do with it 🙃

🫠 The thing is, i opened my fridge in a ravenous state (too much coffee too little breakfast) and had a teenager style "there's absolutely nothing to eat" meltdown

And it's not juuust that there wasnt anything that i WANTED, it was the fact that **i was staring into a FULL messy ass fridge, but still without anything to satisfy my cravings..**

_Soooo, (again nothing to do with my hormones) I went into clear out mode._ 😈

EVERYTHING came out. Drawers were wiped, mystery sauces purged, and hidden gems resurfaced (did you know kimchi like almost never expires!?) **I had fridge-organization-tok (goals), but in the end, settled for a less aesthetic (but still satisfying) cleared out fridge 💅**

**And that's when I saw it.**

[The honey egg recipe my friend sent me last week](https://www.instagram.com/p/DV9A0mIDrxd/).

it was ALL there.
