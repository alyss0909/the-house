# Simmer Email Examples — Index

> Full-text Simmer emails written by Alyssa. Simmer emails are weekly story-led sends that keep the audience warm between promos, with a right-time offer woven in naturally. Unlike the multi-section Soft Sunday newsletter, each Simmer is a single-topic, conversational send — usually 300–600 words. Hook with a personal story, pivot to the business reframe, land the CTA. Read at least one before writing in this format.

## How to use these

**For voice calibration:** Read the opening story paragraph. Notice how fast the personal detail lands and how quickly it pivots to the business parallel. These are not newsletter sections — they flow as one continuous piece.

**For structure reference:** Simmer = story open → reframe / teaching moment → offer → CTA. The offer is woven in, not announced. Keep it conversational, keep it short.

**Rule from examples/INDEX.md:** Always read at least one real example before writing. Never start cold.
