## How To Create A Pretty Link

Having a simple link like www.alyssacoleman.ca/zoom makes your life a lot easier and more on brand, but it's not always that easy when you're sharing links from 3rd party sites like Zoom or sharing a PDF etc. That's why one of my favourite hacks is to use the (free) Pretty Links plugin for WordPress!

Here's a step by step guide on how to get "pretty" branded links:

- **STEP 1:** Log into WordPress >> Plugins >> Add New
- **STEP 2:** Search Plugins >> "Shortlinks by Pretty Links" >> Install now >> Activate
- **STEP 3:** Your new plugin should be ready to use, so head down to your sidebar and click >> Pretty Links >> Add Pretty Link
- **STEP 4:** Choose your target URL
- **STEP 5:** Choose the new slug for your Pretty Link >> Give it a title >> Create!

✺ **Creating a Pretty Link in Squarespace:** You can do this in the URL Mappings section of your Squarespace settings.

## The Double Yes Checklist

By the end of this module, your funnel should be lookin' like it just clocked in for its first shift. Here's what you should have ready for service:

**📁 Your Freebie is Ready to Roll:**
- [ ] You've created your freebie (PDF, quiz, template — whatever you cooked up)
- [ ] You've uploaded it somewhere (Drive, Notion, or your website) and have a shareable URL to send people

**🧲 Your Opt-In Page is Live + Linked:**
- [ ] It's fully designed and has a form ready for signups
- [ ] You've connected it to your email provider so new subscribers immediately get added + tagged
- [ ] You've linked it to your thank you or tripwire page so it automatically pops up right after someone drops their name + email

**🛎️ Your Thank You Page is Good to Go:**
- [ ] It's live and has a clear next step button (like booking a call if that's your move)
- [ ] You've added your scheduler link

**💸 Your Tripwire Sales Page is Doing Her Job:**
- [ ] You've created your low-ticket offer + written the sales page
- [ ] You connected it to your checkout system so people can pay + access right away
- [ ] You've set it to auto tag buyers in your email system

**📬 Your Delivery Emails Are Locked + Loaded:**
- [ ] Freebie delivery email is automated to send immediately
- [ ] Tripwire delivery email is automated to send immediately after purchase
- [ ] Oops email is automated to send 1 day after freebie delivery & filtered to send to everyone EXCEPT tripwire buyers
