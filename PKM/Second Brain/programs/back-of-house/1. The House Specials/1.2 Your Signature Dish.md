## Part 1 — The Story

### a. the sexy title

Brainstorm 3 possible sexy titles for your signature method, are they outcome focused? Is it clear what they will be, do or create if they invest in this method? Come back to this at the end and circle which title you will go with.

### b. the descriptive sentence

Brainstorm a one sentence description of your program in case they literally read nothing else on this page, what is the main outcome of the program? Can you fill in this sentence to create your descriptive subtitle?

The __name of offer__ is a ____what it is____ to help you ___result___

Alternatively, use your PITCH from the last module to act as your descriptive sentence: [name of offer] is a [casual tagline] that helps [these people] get [these results]. Using [philosophy] and [new way]. we've revolutionized [Old way] to get [transformation] without ever having to [avoid these things].

### c. the lemme guess

Let me guess you feel like this, and this, and this…

What is your dream client frustrated with right now? What specific words are they using to describe their problem to their sister, partner, bestie etc? Can you think of one nightmare for EACH phase of your offer?

### d. the but you wish

You wish you felt like this, and this, and imagine this!

Who is your ideal client jealous of? What do they want? What is the heaven-like situation they are dreaming about? Show them you understand their desires. Can you think of one dream outcome for EACH phase of your offer?

### e. well that's exactly why i created this

Well that's exactly why I created ___name of program___. Write one sentence that describes how your signature method is the bridge between how they feel now and how they WANT to feel.

### f. the about you, that's really about them

Why are you the right person for the job? Why are you the BEST person to take them to the outcome they want? What cred do you have that can back that up? What personal experience do you have that makes you understand the method intimately?

## Part 2 — The Offer

### a. the what

Write a quick high-level breakdown of what the program is, add some details here like how many weeks it is or how many modules it is, what it is designed to do, and what outcome it will get them in one or two sentences.

### b. the how

Break down each module, phase, workbook or component of your program. Really SELL each section, add a short outcome focused paragraph or a couple really sexy bullets that get them SO EXCITED to purchase so they can experience all of this.

Think "so that's" and "hawaii" here — tell them about what they COULD find out if they purchased. Note: if you are writing a sales page for a product, swap this section out for an extensive "features" section — here is a great example.

### c. the deliverables

What will they GET if they join? What tangible things can you share? Some great examples are workbooks, calls, community, meal plans, guides.

### d. the call to action

Time to ask for the sale, how will you call your reader to action? Will there be any scarcity? What will it say on your button? What action step do you want them to take?

### e. the bonuses

Will you add bonuses (hint: i vote yes) what can you add that will make their experience even better? Will any of the bonuses disappear? Will this bonus push them over the edge to take action? How will you SELL it?
