# Slow Pinterest
*M3.2 · P3: Traffic Pantry*

> [!abstract] Quick Read
> **What's here:** A four-part setup and optimization checklist for building an evergreen Pinterest presence — account setup, keyword research, SEO configuration, and page/pin optimization.

## Part 1: Set Up

- [ ] **Convert or create a Business account** — Search "how to convert to a Pinterest business account" on YouTube and follow the latest tutorial
- [ ] **Claim your website domain** — Search: how to claim my [website platform] website on pinterest
- [ ] **Turn your profile visibility on** — In Settings > Search Privacy, toggle visibility on

---

## Part 2: Keywords

**Map your 4–5 core pillars' keywords**

- [ ] **Brainstorm problem aware searches (10+)** — Ie: how to be more productive
- [ ] **Brainstorm solution aware searches (10+)** — Ie: how to start time blocking
- [ ] **Brainstorm brand aware searches (5+)** — Ie: how to time block in notion

- [ ] **Use the Pinterest search bar** — Type each pillar term, and record every autocomplete suggestion. Press Enter, and note every bubble under "More ideas like this"
- [ ] **Capture related searches** — Scroll your feed, and jot down "related-searches" bubbles. Use a combination of the 2 to create varying long tail keyword combinations

**Examples:**
- minimalist interior design
- 80s interior design aesthetic
- Scandinavian kitchen design mood board

- [ ] **Check seasonality with Pinterest Trends tool** — If skincare routine peaks in January, plan pins for October–November
- [ ] **Get search volumes via Ads Manager** — Go to Ads → Create Campaign → Manual Campaign → Get Started → Brand Awareness → Continue → Choose Your Own → Interests and Keywords → Add Keywords
- [ ] **Audit competitors** — Search your keywords, and analyze top pins for visuals, fonts, angles, keywords in titles/descriptions and use that to refine your own approach

## Part 3: SEO

- [ ] **Optimize your Profile Name (≤65 chars)** — Use your name or brand + 1 spicy keyword. Make it clear what you create content about, not your job title. (e.g. Alyssa Coleman | productivity for entrepreneurs)

- [ ] **Rewrite Your Bio (≤500 chars)** — Include:
  - Who you serve
  - How you solve their problems
  - Weave in 5 long-tail keywords
  - What kind of content you create
  - Why someone should follow you + soft CTA
  - Formula: *Helping [your people] [solve a specific problem] | [keywords] | [your vibe/CTA]*

- [ ] **Upload Your Profile Photo** — Use the same one from your other platforms. Choose a clear, scroll-stopping pic of you (not your logo)

- [ ] **Design a Header Image (1600 x 900 px)** — This is your profile's first impression. Brand photo + bold overlay text. Promo graphic for your freebie or offer.

- [ ] **Create 5 Boards**
  - 4 boards matching your 4 pillars
  - 1 board for your freebie/tripwire/offer keywords
  - Title each board with a seed or long-tail keyword

- [ ] **Write Board Descriptions (≤500 chars)** — Include:
  - Hook + benefit
  - What's inside
  - What it solves
  - 5 long-tail keywords
  - Soft CTA
  - Formula: *Need [solution or idea]? This board is full of [keywords/phrases] to help you [achieve goal]. From [example content] to [another example], you'll find all the inspiration here.*

- [ ] **Design clear Board Covers** — branded image + keyword overlay text
- [ ] **Prime each board** — Pin 10–15 high-performing, relevant pins so Pinterest "learns" your focus

## Part 4: Optimize

### Optimize Blog Posts

- [ ] Use vertical (pinnable) images
- [ ] Add keywords in the alt text of images
- [ ] Save image files with keywords in the .jpg file name
- [ ] Optimize blog post titles for search by using long tail solution focused keywords — keep it under 60 characters
- [ ] Use H2s with supporting keywords
- [ ] Break up your content into clear chunks with H2 headings that incorporate supporting keywords in that content pillar (Pinterest scans these for context, so it knows who to show it to)
- [ ] Grow your email list with a lead magnet
- [ ] Optimize meta description with your primary keyword at the beginning of the sentence
- [ ] Customize the URL slug to include keyword — yourdomain.com/email-funnel-tips

### Optimize Opt-in & Tripwire Pages

- [ ] Keyword in the title of the page
- [ ] Use a clear, keyword-rich headline
- [ ] Keyword in the URL slug
- [ ] Optimized meta description
- [ ] Make the promise clear in the first 75 characters
- [ ] Vertical, pinnable mockup image
- [ ] Keyword in the file name
- [ ] Keyword in the image alt text

### Optimize Sales Page

- [ ] Use a clear, keyword-rich headline
- [ ] Include any benefit keywords and audience keywords
- [ ] Meta title should include the offer TYPE & niche keywords (ie: notion template course for business coaches)
