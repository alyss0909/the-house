## The Power of a Well-Executed Sequence

Your emails should mirror the buyer's belief journey. A well-executed sequence:

- Builds relationship with your community
- Shifts their perspective to your solution
- Creates solution awareness
- Makes an impact by solving your dream client's problems NOW

---

## The Documentary Framework

The documentary is the mental model for the whole sequence. These emails are where you really form a bond with your community (know, like, trust), so you want to write emails that will show them:

- A little more about you and how you've overcome similar problems to the ones they're facing now (or helped your clients to)
- That you create open-worthy content that's valuable to them (share links to your most popular posts or write emails that solve their biggest problems)
- Use this time to invite people into other parts of your community (like socials, your podcast, Pinterest, etc) that A: provides VALUE and B: NOURISHES

**The three-part structure:**

| Part | Name | Email Types |
|---|---|---|
| Part One | The Symptoms | Your Story, Ask Questions |
| Part Two | The Diagnosis | Bullets – Value Add, Steps – Value Add, Case Study – Aha Moment, Redirect |
| Part Three | The Prescription | Logic Focused Sale, Sales Page Copy Close |

Choose at least two of each across the buyer journey.

---

## Part One — The Symptoms

Paint a picture of their problem. Start to highlight some of the other symptoms they might not have considered yet — like googling your symptoms to understand what's actually going on.

**The Awareness Scale:**

| Stage | Description |
|---|---|
| Stage 1 | Unaware |
| Stage 2 | Pain Aware |
| Stage 3 | Solution Aware |
| Stage 4 | Product Aware |

### The "About You" That's Really About Them

This is NOT "your story." It's short and sweet. This is YOUR CRED — why you are the RIGHT person to help them get the outcome they want.

- 1 part personality, 1 part credibility
- If your dream client doesn't see themselves in sentence one, they won't read sentence two.

---

## Part Two — The Diagnosis

They will not BUY if they don't believe the diagnosis. Give them QUICK WINS here.

The job of the diagnosis emails:
- Belief shift
- Diagnose
- Give a win & tease the solution
- Paint the picture of the outcomes (& dream transformation)

### The Buy-In

Get very clear on the root cause of their problem (the bulk of the documentary). Shift their belief (aha moments). Pair value-focused emails — using bullets and steps to solve problems — with a vulnerable story. Future pace / paint the picture of nightmare & dream: THESE symptoms, THIS is what we did, THIS is the result.

### Example Email — "How to Stop Being So Damn BUSY"

> **SUBJECT: how to stop being so damn BUSY**
>
> Tell me if this rings a bell {{ subscriber.first_name }} ……
>
> When your friends ask "how have you been?" you pretty much always respond:
>
> "Omg, I've been SO busy" *[symptom]*
>
> And that ain't a lie, you're spending pretty much all your time on this passion project, your business is your baby, but there's a nagging feeling that even though you're spending ALL DAMN DAY at your desk… you aren't really getting anywhere?? *[very specific examples of those symptoms showing up in their everyday life]*
>
> Ya girl, i've been there *[create a connection]*
>
> I was super fkn busy all day long, I worked nights and weekends and ate lunch at my desk .. but the cold hard truth is….
>
> I wasn't being productive at all. *[vulnerable share & belief shift]*
>
> And as soon as I implemented some very strategic business moves i suddenly became *[tease the solution]*
>
> A LOT LESS BUSY *[paint the picture of the transformation]*
>
> And here's the fun part - I suddenly had a lot more time to work on that passion project, or to ACTUALLY take that road trip with my boyfriend (and you better believe we took the scenic route) *[paint the picture of the transformation]*
>
> If you're ready to stop being SO DAMN BUSY all the time, and finally implement something you can rely on - then here's what i want you to do: *[establish authority]*
>
> - Do this *[quick win]*
> - Do this *[quick win]*
> - Do this *[quick win]*
>
> Plus, if you want to take this one step further, I have a youtube video here. *[Call to action]*
>
> Because YOU deserve to be the one living the life of YOUR DREAMS.
>
> P.S. *[repeat the call to action/helpful thing for ppl who skim emails]*

### The Core Insight

> Most people buy because they finally believe they're ready. Not because your offer got better, or they read the perfect bonus. Your sequence's job is to build belief in them, not just sell belief in you.
>
> When they finally believe they're ready, the yes feels obvious. Your job isn't to convince, it's to remind them of who they already are.

---

## Part Three — The Prescription

Start to get very specific about your solution and your philosophy on solving their problem. This is the bridge — where you overtly share your offer, booking a call, etc. Use your best copy from your sales page.

> The first email makes them feel seen... the last one should make them feel certain.
> Certain they're in the right place. Certain you're the one. Certain it's time.

---

## Keep in Mind

- This is a STOCK sequence (not related to ONE freebie)
- Clear call to action in every email
- Always add a P.S. — reiterate for skimmers + CTA
- Small paragraphs
- Conversational copy
