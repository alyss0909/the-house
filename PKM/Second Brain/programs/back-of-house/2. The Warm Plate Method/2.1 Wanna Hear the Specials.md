# Wanna Hear the Specials?
*M2.1 · P2: The Warm Plate Method*

> [!abstract] Quick Read
> **What's here:** A three-part brainstorm framework for mining your story, philosophy, and IP into the raw material that powers your warm welcome email sequence.
> **Key insight:** Every email in the sequence maps to a stage of the medical metaphor — symptoms, diagnosis, prescription — so subscribers feel seen *before* they've said a word.
> **Surfaces in:** Welcome sequence writing, discovery call prep, sales page storytelling, and any teaching moment where Alyssa walks clients through positioning their own expertise.

## Part 1: Symptoms

**YOUR STORY:** Think back to a time when you were in your dream client's shoes. What did you experience that relates to where they are right now, BEFORE they get to your signature dish?

*Pull from: ✺ Why you created your business ✺ Mission statement*

---

**WEIRD SYMPTOMS:** What are some of the very specific examples of those symptoms showing up in your / a client's everyday life? Think: weird, subtle, overlooked symptoms of their problem (ex. You kept leaving your keys in the fridge, crying in a Starbucks drive thru, canceling plans for the 3rd time, refreshing analytics at 1am)

*✺ Use: BUYER PERSONA Dream & nightmare section*

---

**COPING MECHANISMS:** What are the strange or slightly embarrassing things your clients (or you!) have done to cope with this problem? Think: What habits do they have that are actually sneaky coping mechanisms? (Ex: over planning, always using a link in bio instead of their website)

*✺ Use: WHAT BROUGHT THEM TO YOUR BUSINESS — Flip your answers here; what "habits" are actually sneaky symptoms?*

---

**ODD CONNECTION:** What's one symptom your dream client definitely feels but wouldn't think is connected to the deeper issue? (ex: I thought my problem was procrastination, but it was actually decision fatigue.)

*✺ Use: WHAT'S HOLDING THEM BACK in WHAT BROUGHT THEM TO YOUR BUSINESS. Find a belief or habit they think is the problem but actually isn't.*

---

**THE BRIDGE:** What are some of the key shifts (or first small steps) you made during your own transformation? Think: a perspective change, a habit, a turning point that lets them know *you get it*

*✺ Use: GROWTH + THE MAIN TRANSFORMATIONS in your signature offer. What first steps or mindset shifts helped you or clients change direction?*

---

**THE RESOLUTION:** What does your life look like now that you've overcome those symptoms? Paint the picture with examples and specifics they'd relate to.

*✺ Use: THE MAIN TRANSFORMATIONS + WHAT MAKES YOUR METHOD DIFFERENT. Paint a picture of life after your offer. Pull specifics from what your client gets.*

---

**EXPERT QUESTIONS:** What are some "common thread" questions that you'd ask your dream client about? When you go to the doctor and they start to ask things like, "does it hurt when I do this" or "have you been experiencing headaches?" If the questions are accurate, it establishes them as an expert and you start to think, "Wow, this person must know something about my diagnosis because they're talking about my symptoms before I've even mentioned them." You instantly trust them. Brainstorm some provocative, fun questions to help them self identify because they have ALL these symptoms!

*✺ Use: BUYER PERSONA + WHAT ARE THEIR STRUGGLES, GOALS, AND QUIRKS. Pull common threads, use this section to reverse-engineer the questions only you would know to ask.*

## Part 2: Diagnose

**BELIEF SHIFT:** What is one belief shift your dream client NEEDS to have in order to see their problem from your perspective — meaning that your philosophy is HOW they should solve their problem? (ex: they think they need to count calories, but they actually need to support their hormones) People think they know the problem. You show them they've been fighting the wrong battle.

*✺ Use: WHAT MAKES YOUR METHOD DIFFERENT — This is where your core belief flips their whole approach. Tie it to your disruption.*

---

**LIGHTBULB MOMENT:** What's one aha-moment your clients often have that completely shifts how they see their situation?

*✺ Use: WHAT TRANSFORMATIONS THEY EXPERIENCE. Look for any big moment where they *got it.* Highlight the before & after moment.*

---

**HOT TAKE:** What's your controversial opinion or *hot take* on how this problem should be solved, ideally something that most people in your niche won't say out loud, or is totally contradictory to the "common" answer to their problem?

*✺ Use: WHAT ASPECTS OF THEIR BUSINESS DO YOU DISAGREE WITH? (under Competitive Analysis) — This is your mic drop moment, be disruptive here baby (but usually avoid naming names, unless you're REALLY feelin spicy hehe)*

---

**SYMPTOM SOOTHER:** Choose a specific symptom. When did you realize that the REAL problem of that symptom was DEEPER. How can you NOW give them a quick win associated with that deeper problem? (write out 2-3 steps to give them). Once you / your client did those things, what outcomes did you experience?

*✺ Use: WHAT'S HOLDING THEM BACK + FREEBIE QUICK WIN. Take one of those sneaky root issues, and walk them through a fix. Think step-by-step.*

*Repeat above for 3-4 symptoms.*

---

**CASE STUDY:** Give an example of a client (you can also choose someone well known in your industry) that demonstrates the symptoms, diagnosis, and the prescription. Use this example to allow your dream client to envision themselves having this same transformation — ONLY focus on the parts of the story that are Them-Centric, things they can LEARN or have an aha moment from.

*✺ Use: CLIENT EXAMPLES from Impact Section (or create one from your Buyer Persona). Don't overdo the bio; just show the transformation they'll care about.*

---

**VALUABLE REDIRECTS:** Do you already have some great blogs, podcasts, social posts, youtube videos, captions, webinars, random notes that you could SEND people to in an email that would help them get MORE value and take next steps?

*✺ Use: VALUABLE CONTENT under Funnel Decisions. Point them toward your blogs, videos, or any content you've already made that helps deepen the aha (if you don't have any yet, you can use this moment to plan something you WANNA make & put it in your calendar).*

## Part 3: Prescription

**PHILOSOPHY:** What is your philosophy on the root cause of the symptoms your dream client currently has?

*✺ Use: WHAT IS YOUR PHILOSOPHY OR PROCESS? + WHAT MAKES YOUR PRODUCT DIFFERENT. Re-state your core truth. This is your lens on why the problem exists.*

---

**THE FACTS:** What are 5 scary pieces of evidence (i.e. facts, statistics, etc) that illustrate how bad the symptoms are, how many people are experiencing the same problem, or how much your solution improved / can improve their situation.

---

**POP CULTURE PROOF:** What are some ways their symptoms show up or can be referenced within pop culture? Ie: Chappel Roan was playing Pink Pony Club in deli's before she changed THIS BRANDING TECHNIQUE and then she headlined Coachella with the same song. / Here's the ONE thing that X/Y/Z celebs all have in common: they became sex symbols IMMEDIATELY after their stylist changed this ONE thing in their wardrobe. Or their publicists ALL trained them to say THIS in interviews (show examples.)

*✺ Use what you wrote under COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS or WHAT DO YOU WANT THEM TO FEEL? as creative fuel.*

---

**WEIRD TECHNIQUE:** What part of your process would your dream client be most surprised to learn actually matters? (the weirder, more shocking or counter-intuitive the better).

*✺ Use: WHAT MAKES YOUR METHOD DIFFERENT. Pick the part of your process that no one expects but is weirdly powerful.*

---

**THE METAPHOR:** What's your favorite metaphor or analogy that explains how your process works in a way anyone could understand?

*✺ Use: YOUR ELEVATOR PITCH or TAGLINE. Turn one of your process steps into a metaphor or comparison that would make sense to a fifth grader.*

---

**THE DETAILS:** Give it to them straight. What 2 things do they need to hear, objectively, in order to understand YOUR philosophy & method.

*✺ Use: WHAT MAKES YOUR PRODUCT DIFFERENT + ONE SENTENCE OFFER. Reiterate the unique method and outcome.*

---

**THE FAQ:** What are the 3 main things your dream client might be worried about when it comes to booking a call or buying? Call out the objections before they happen. (ex: Think this only works if you have a big audience? Here's how Leah went from 211 followers to 3 paying clients)

*✺ Use: BUYER PERSONA & use "What's holding them back" or "What they wish they had". Pre-answer the 3 questions they're definitely thinking but haven't said yet.*

---

**THE URGENCY:** What's changing in your industry, market, or buyer's life that makes NOW the best time to act?

*✺ Bonus points: Can you answer this question: Most people wait until [this] happens before they take this seriously, but by then it's already cost them [this thing they want to avoid].*
