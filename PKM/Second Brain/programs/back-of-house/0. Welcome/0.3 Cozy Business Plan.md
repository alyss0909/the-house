# **YOUR PERSON**

**DREAM CLIENT**  
Who is your ideal client? What are their struggles, goals, and quirks that make them *your people?*

### **WHAT MAKES THEM, THEM?**

Where do they shop, what books do they read, what do they do in their spare time, What are their hobbies? (this will come in handy later when you're looking to target them with keywords or ads)

### **WHAT BROUGHT THEM TO YOUR BUSINESS?**

How can you help them? What are their future goals? What keeps them up at night? what do they wish they had, and what is holding them back from having that?

When people interact with you or your business, what do you want them to ***feel***?

**BUYER PERSONA:**  
Let's summarize these things into your dream client's buyer persona (we'll use this throughout the course)

**Here's an example** *(Back of House's buyer persona):*  
*Back of House is for **service providers and coaches** who are amazing at delivering 1:1 services, leading group programs, or selling courses but **hate constantly chasing leads**. Many of them **currently rely on social media** or word of mouth to find clients and build their audience but are **looking for a more sustainable way to bring in consistent leads and sales** every month. They're incredible at serving their clients and delivering value, **but struggle to market their services in a way that feels aligned, and without being glued to their phones.** They want to **increase their monthly revenue** with **steady, predictable systems**, but they often find themselves **scrambling to create content,** and **worrying about where their next client will come from.** They're creative, introverted, and perfectionistic, **craving more time for big ideas,** deep work, and living the softceo life. They're tired of feeling like they're **running on fumes** and want a business system that **feels calm**, intentional, and just works.*

# **THE NUMBERS**

PROFIT GOAL EXERCISE

Make a giant list of all the things you would LOVE to have in your life, no matter how crazy they might be… personal chef? A PR manager? Virtual assistant, $x amount to spend on ads, this much travel, new couch etc..

Put a $$ amount next to it (don't forget the boring things like rent, groceries, etc) then you start to get a rough estimate of what your dream life looks like and how much it will cost to live that dream life!

1. The income you want to earn from this offer per month:

### **DIVIDED BY:**

**B.** number of clients you want to see at a time (if applicable)

### **MULTIPLIED BY:**

**C.** length of your signature method in months (ie: 3 months or 1.5 months):

### **= IDEAL PRICE**

| A \_\_\_ ÷ B\_\_\_\_ x C \_\_\_\_ \= $ \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ |
| ----- |

**PRODUCTIVE NOTE:** Decide the type of income you want to make THEN figure out how to make your product worth as much as you want to charge. **You can do one of two things:** increase your sales ie: number of clients you take on at a time which also includes increasing your leads; OR you can increase the amount you charge for your product by making it more valuable.

# **CONFIDENTLY INCREASING YOUR PRICE**

**IF YOU'RE PLANNING TO INCREASE YOUR PRICE I WANT YOU TO ASK YOURSELF TWO QUESTIONS.**

What is the transformation I'm promising? The one they really want?

How can I get them to that as quickly and EASILY (least work for them) as possible?

**PRODUCTIVE NOTE:** More isn't always MORE, but sometimes you can add in a few more workbooks, or trainings, or bonuses, or gifts etc if that will **help them get there sooner.** However, sometimes LESS is actually more valuable to your client \- **the more you take off their plate the more they are willing to pay** \- play with these two ideas and think about some ways to make your product worth as much as you want to charge.

### **BONUS QUESTION:**

What can I do to be ready to charge that much?

**PRODUCTIVE NOTE:** You already know there are lots of people charging as much as you want to, and MORE! The difference is usually just your belief about that $ amount, so do some work here to get yourself feeling ready to charge that price if it's feeling off right now.

# **FREEBIE**

**What are 3 ways you can pre-qualify someone to be the RIGHT fit for your main offer?** *(Think: specific pain points, life stage, team size, current income, experience level, symptoms)*

How can you make your freebie a quick win for your ideal client WHILE making sure that ONLY people who are the right fit for your main offer would want it?

***Ex:** nutritionist with a focus on healing digestive issues with a parasite cleanse. She could EASILY attract a wide range of people who want to heal their digestion & bloating but who could NOT be dealing with parasites, they could be lactose intolerant or have leaky gut etc. If she's not careful she could have a list FULL of people with digestion probs who NEVER want her parasite protocol.*

Brainstorm some good freebie ideas now (you'll narrow down later). Will it be a quiz, PDF, workbook, meditation, video from you, webinar, quiz or something else? What will the main outcome of the freebie be? *Note: there are a lot of TRASH freebies out there & SO MUCH free info on line \- make sure this ACTUALLY cool.*

# **TRIPWIRE**

How can you go EVEN deeper into your ideal client's journey and pre-qualify them even MORE? What's one done for you type solution you could offer as a low ticket product that would GUARANTEE anyone who bought this would be PERFECT for your core offer?

What is the main goal of your tripwire? How can you help to give your ideal client the outcome they want from your lead magnet but quicker and easier?

**PRODUCTIVE NOTE:** be careful not to make your core offer obsolete to people who purchase this, think of it like a little bread & olive oil app \- suddenly you realize how hungry you really are, and you're READY to order.

What price point would work for your audience? (just guess!)
