*M0.4 · P0: Welcome & Prep Work*

---

> [!abstract] Quick Read
> **What's here:** A quarterly review and forecast tool — looks back at the last 90 days (list growth, sales, expenses) and sets targets for the next quarter. Includes links to Alyssa's tracking spreadsheets and a social tracker tutorial.
> **Key insight:** The framework pairs debrief with forecast AND emotional check-in — "Does this excite you? Is this realistic?" signals that Alyssa's approach to goal-setting is as much about alignment as math.

## QUARTER DEBRIEF & FORECAST

How much has your list grown in the last 90 days?

How many sales calls have you had within the last 90 days?

How many new sales in the last 90 days?

How much did you spend in software / biz expenses last quarter?

## THE QUARTER FORECAST

What's your income target?

How many new leads do you need?

How many sales calls should you book?

How do you feel about hitting these targets? Is this realistic? Does this excite you?

## SOCIAL GROWTH WORKSHEET

Social community is a huge part of growing your online presence and warming potential clients up to your brand (and you). It's important to track the growth on these platforms not only so that we know that we're having continued success, but also to decipher which platforms we should be spending the majority of our energy on. This is why you want to track how your audience is growing over time.

I've created a social media tracker to help you keep tabs on your social growth. So make a copy of this, and add the link to your calendar once per month so you remember to fill it out!
