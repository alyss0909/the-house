## Post Launch Blues Note

YESSSS, you made it! 🥂

Before we get into the debrief, let's talk really quickly about Post Launch Blues. Here's the thing, you just went through a FULL LAUNCH, you met your upper limits and pushed through your comfort zones. That's a lot of energy, and it's totally normal to feel ALL THE THINGS right now. Also, you just did a lot of SHOWING UP so if you're feeling the "PLBs" take a beat and take care of yourself. Get offline, Journal it out, or just have a little Netflix binge guilt free!

Whether your launch CRUSHES or FLOPS, this debrief is all about taking a step back, taking care of yourself then coming back to the numbers from an unemotional, soft CEO headspace to come back bigger and badder for your next launch.

MOST IMPORTANTLY: don't get hung up or stressed out here — I know it can seem like alot to recap the whole launch, but now is the BEST and most valuable time to debrief while everything is fresh in your mind. Don't think about it too much, just get it down on paper and move on. You'll thank yourself next launch!
