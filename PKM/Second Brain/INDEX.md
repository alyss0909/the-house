# Second Brain

> Alyssa's personal copy and content library. Every word, framework, and template needed to produce content that sounds like her — not like an AI that read her once.

---

## START HERE → every session, every task

**[`COMPASS.md`](COMPASS.md)**

This is the single entry point for all Second Brain work. It tells you exactly which files to load for your task type in ~100 lines — no reading the full routing guide to find out what to read. Open COMPASS before anything else.

For copy tasks, COMPASS routes you through `analysis/voice-profile.md` first (the full calibration in ~3,000 tokens), then to the 1–2 task-specific files you actually need. For BOH program content, COMPASS routes to `programs/back-of-house/BOH-COMPASS.md`.

For full routing rationale and decision notes: `analysis/copy-task-routing.md`.

## What each folder is for — and when to open it

| Folder / File | What it is | Open it when... |
|---|---|---|
| `COMPASS.md` | **Navigation entry point** — 100-line routing guide for every task type. Always first. | Before opening any other file |
| `me.md` | Identity anchor — who she is, what she sells, her 5 voice words, origin story, restaurant metaphor | Quick factual lookup only (voice-profile.md supersedes for copy tasks) |
| `analysis/` | **What we learned** from studying her real work — voice patterns, page analysis, what performed and why | Before writing, to calibrate voice and task approach |
| `examples/` | **Her actual words** — verbatim copy exactly as she wrote it | To feel the real voice before writing; never skip |
| `frameworks/` | **The rules extracted** — archetypes, structures, hook formulas, decision logic | To find the right format/approach for the task |
| `templates/` | **Fill in the blanks** — pre-built skeletons with [brackets] for every content type | To start writing without a blank page |
| `programs/` | **Course content** — BOH modules, webinars, transcripts, slide decks | For deep context on what she teaches and how she explains it |

**The gut-check before every output:** Does this sound like Alyssa, or like a content creator who read Alyssa once?
