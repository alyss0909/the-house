# Easiest Email = Highest Return

## What keeps showing up

A persistent insight that the emails Alyssa sends without overthinking — the "weeknight meal" emails, the ones that feel almost too simple — outperform the elaborate ones. Not just in her own experience. In the data. This keeps appearing in different forms: as product positioning for a newsletter offer, as a hook formula, as a teaching principle inside BOH, and as something she has strong feelings about relative to what most email marketing gurus teach.

The underlying claim is quietly radical: the email that's *easy to send consistently* beats the email that's theoretically perfect but never gets sent.
