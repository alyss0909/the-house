# Emails

*[[business]] · [[promos]] · [[offers]] · [[a-year-of-emails]] · [[the-5-minute-newsletter]]*

---

**What this covers:** Everything to do with email in my business — weekly simmer sends, right-time offers, Soft Sundays, sequences, and anything else that keeps my list tight and my marketing on autopilot.

**What good looks like:** A back of house for my list — emails written once that run and convert long after I send them. Weekly send is breezy, sequences are dialled in, and the list feels like an asset, not a chore.

**What I am ignoring:** Launch copy, sales pages, and campaign-specific promo strategy — that's [[promos]]. The products I sell *about* email (YOE, 5MN) live in [[offers]].
