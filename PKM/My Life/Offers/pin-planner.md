# Pin Planner

**Part of:** [[socials]]

## What it is

**"Swipe the slow marketing strategy that turns 4 pieces of content into daily traffic, while you do literally anything else."**

**Tier:** Freebie | **Theme:** Pinterest | **Price:** Free

A Notion-based Pinterest content calendar for planning, scheduling, and batching pins — so your best content works on Pinterest's search engine for months (even years).

Business owners who want to use Pinterest to grow their email list but don't know how to set it up strategically.
