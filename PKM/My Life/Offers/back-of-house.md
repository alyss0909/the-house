# Back of House

**Part of:** [[business]]
**Related project:** [[spring-26-launch]]

## What it is

**"The automated sales & marketing department for CEOs with better things to do."**

**Tier:** Flagship program | **Theme:** BOH systems | **Price:** $3,000

A set-it-up-once system for Pinterest-to-email traffic, evergreen sequences, and a sales mechanism that runs while you're off doing literally anything else. Restaurant metaphor throughout.

## Who it's for

Online business owners — coaches, course creators, service providers — who are done feeding the algorithm and want a marketing system that compounds and converts on autopilot.
