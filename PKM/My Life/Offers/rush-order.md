## What it is

**"Back of House on 2x speed."**

**Tier:** Done-with-you upsell / standalone offer | **Theme:** BOH systems | **Price:** $2,999

A 6-week, high-touch experience where Alyssa builds a custom Pinterest + email marketing system tailored to the client's specific niche, audience, and offers. Positioned as an OTO inside the Back of House funnel and/or as a standalone VIP experience.

No cookie-cutter strategies. Just a bespoke blueprint + implementation support.
