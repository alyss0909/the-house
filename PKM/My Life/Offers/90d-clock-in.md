# 90d Clock In

**Part of:** [[business]]

## What it is

**"Turn your existing offers, leads, and ideas into finished assets, booked clients, and time-and-a-half paycheques."**

**Tier:** Low ticket | **Theme:** Productivity | **Price:** $19

A Notion dashboard for a focused 90-day sprint — one department at a time, close open loops, and turn loose ideas into assets that keep working long after the sprint ends.

## Who it's for

Entrepreneurs who want to actually finish what they start over a quarter, without the harsh hustle energy.

The notion dashboard designed to turn your existing offers, leads, and ideas into finished assets, booked clients, and time and a half paycheques.

Double down on your good ideas, pick up the right shifts, clock in now, & let future you collect later

Planning Season Sale!

**What's Inside:**

90 Day Shift Dashboard: Your home base for the next 90 days. This is how you'll stay focused on your goals (no distractions) and see exactly what you're working toward in this sprint

Step by Step Planning Flow: Unload all your (good) ideas, sort them into the right department, and assign them to the right month

Department Tagging System: Organize your work across offers, leads, sales, and content so everything ties back to revenue

Shift Queue + Smart Weekly Views: Once you've set your goals and unloaded all your best ideas, you'll see one master task system with filtered weekly and category views so your work organizes itself

Monthly + Weekly Shift Pages: Access Pre-built focus spaces that connect your big picture plan to your actual week (no more overthinking)

Idea Pantry & Open Loop Capture: A dedicated space to capture every stray million dollar idea so nothing gets lost or distracts you mid-shift

Closing Shift Log: Wrap, review, and carry forward what's working into next shift

**Why service pro's love it:**

Focused: Choose ONE department at a time and go all in... you know exactly what you're doing so you just show up and follow through

Intentional: A system that doesn't rely on you keeping track of...literally everything. Designed to clean up loose ends, close open loops (and open tabs)..finally

Compounding: The work you're doing turns into assets and systems that keep working (for you) long after your 90 day sprint
