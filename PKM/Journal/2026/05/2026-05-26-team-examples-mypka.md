# Team Examples -- MyPKA

Ideas for AI team roles in a creator context and a parent context.

## The Creator's Team

Drafter (turns rough thoughts into long-form drafts), Editor (sharpens drafts), Domain Researcher (audience-deep), Designer (covers, headers, social graphics), Video Editor (when video shows up), Community Manager (audience interactions), Calendar Owner (publishing schedule). Seven added over six to twelve months. Eleven total.

## The Parent's Team

Calendar Owner, Meal Planner, School Comms, Activity Coordinator, Travel Planner.
