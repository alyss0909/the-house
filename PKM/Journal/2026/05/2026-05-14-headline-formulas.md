## A/B testing recipe

1. Test two formulas from *different buckets*
2. Use identical audience, offer, and timeframe
3. Minimum sample: 1,000+ visitors per variant
4. Measure click-through or opt-in rate (not vanity metrics)
5. Winner becomes the control; test next formula against it
