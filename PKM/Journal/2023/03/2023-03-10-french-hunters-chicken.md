# French Hunter's Chicken
https://vikalinka.com/chicken-chasseur-recipe/
2 tbsp olive oil
3 -4 lbs skinless boneless chicken breasts
2 tbsp butter
2 medium onion sliced
500g/16oz mushrooms cut in half
4 cloves garlic sliced
2 tbsp tomato paste
250ml/1 cup dry white wine
8 fresh thyme sprigs leaves only
4 fresh bay leaves
300 g/1 cup canned diced tomatoes
700ml/ 3 cups chicken stock
Olives dude 
🔪RECIPE: 🔪
1) season chicken in smoke paprika/harissa/delicious spice blend, helps with browning and all around flavour
1) In a large pan heat 1 tbsp of oil and brown chicken breasts seasoned with salt and pepper on both sides until golden. You may have to do it in batches to avoid overcrowding. Remove to a plate and set aside until needed. 
2) To the same pan add butter, sliced onions, mushrooms and garlic and saute with a pinch of salt on low heat for 10 minutes.
3) Then add the white wine and let it bubble away for a couple of minutes. 
4) Then add the thyme leaves, bay leaves and tomato paste. Bring the chicken breasts back to the pan together with diced tomatoes and chicken stock, 
5) bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer covered for 20
