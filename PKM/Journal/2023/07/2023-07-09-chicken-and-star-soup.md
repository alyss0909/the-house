# Chicken and star soup
Sauté:
Onions 
Celery
Carrots 
Little bit of garlic 
In olive oil 
Brown chicken cubes (I make them small... spoon size) 
With salt and pepper & paprika
Add chicken broth 
When boiling add cubed potatoes and pasta stars
When potatoes cooked soup is ready. 
I like to add collard greens bc I love them. But Vavo says that's not chicken and star soup then...
