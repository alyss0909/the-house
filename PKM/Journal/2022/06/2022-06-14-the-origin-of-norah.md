# The origin of Norah
Stretch and sweep #2 at 145pm on Sunday
First contractions started at 245
Labored at home with consistent contractions until 1230am on Monday
Puked a bunch after the especially painful contractions 
Arrived at hospital at 1am 4cm dialated
Intense contractions with laughing gas until 6am only 5cm dialated
Epidural (major relief) 6am Monday
Now Ryan will tell most of the story lol 
Alyssa got her first bit of sleep
Contraction slowed down, but were still extremely painful and epidural was not working properly on one side so Alyssa had to be topped up several times to keep getting some relief 6cm dialated
Requested oxytocin from on-call OB to chemically start up contractions again (midwife's don't administer oxytocin) at 1pm on Monday 13th 
Marta went home to rest and new nurse Kristin who we thought we wouldn't like turned out to be a real MVP and Alyssa finally got lots of relief and some sleep from topping up drugs the correct way 
 430pm 6-7cm dialated finally some progress
10:30pm Monday: pushing started
Pause pushing
We find out baby is sunny side up from OB and she tries to flip her a couple times but she knows what she likes and flips right back up, we also find out there is a small part of the ?
Talks started about C-section in a discouraging way
More intense left hip pain where the epidural wasn't working
I started pushing again once there was a serious pressure in tailbone with Alyssa's favourite nurse Sandra
