# Tomato Sauce
Wash jars 
Wash tomatoes 
Cut up
Boil w cup of water
Take extra water off with collander 
Crank 3x
Boil
Salt
Jar
___________________________
Lessons from this year: 
We needed 3 of the big bins. 
4+ home Depot buckets
Set up the outdoor situation night before
Have all bins washed & ready 
Low jar / beer can boxes are ideal for holding/ moving all the jars (lots)
Would've been good to have a 2nd jar tongs
Get water boiling for tomatoes asap & dw about "releasing liquid" just soften them and get movin
Bigger strainer (like a laundry basket)
Actual pot racks or silicone rings for stacking jars in the water bath
Plan ahead and buy seals from Amazon in bulk
This year we bought 6 bushels for $216 and filled x jars
201 (+24 waste)
