# Ah, inspiration.
Ah, inspiration.
The muse of artists,
The siren call of dreamers,
And the achilles heel of entrepreneurs.
⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀
If not done right, the search for inspiration can be unbelievably counterproductive.
⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀
So while everyone is setting their new year’s diet resolutions… I’m trying my own
⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀
Keto diet? Paleo diet? Plant based diet?
⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀
Nah, I’m on the inspiration diet.
⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀
Step one: Take stock of who TRULY inspires you.
Step two: ELIMINATE everything else, unfollow, unsubscribe, unfriend.
⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀
Sometimes we have to detox our cupboards (and lives) of the JUNK so that we’ll only have healthy options🗑️
⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀
Reminder: YOU are your own greatest inspiration.
⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀
