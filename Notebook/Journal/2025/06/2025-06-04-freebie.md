🔁 3. SEQUENCE HUB
A gallery or board view where each card = a plug-and-play sequence (you only promised 3, bless you)

Description of when to use it
5 curated prompts pulled from the master list (linked)
Suggested subject lines
CTA examples for that flow
Swaps for different vibes (e.g., “Want a softer version? Use X instead of Y”)
