**[How To Write A Persuasive Email](https://bdow.com/stories/persuasive-email/)**

**[250+ Trigger Words](https://bdow.com/stories/trigger-words/)**
Massive catalog of emotionally charged words organized by category — urgency, fear, curiosity, trust, etc. Covers headlines, subject lines, buttons, pop-ups, landing pages. Real examples from brands. Good inspo for a "words that convert" style post.
