- Carousel copy for all top 9, slide by slide -- turn into a template
- My website copy

**5 codes worth knowing:**
- `ULTRATHINK` -- makes Claude think harder on complex problems
- `/caveman` -- cuts 75% of output tokens instantly
- `/insights` -- analyzes your sessions and suggests how to be more productive
- `/loop` -- schedules a task to repeat automatically
- `/btw` -- answers without canceling its current work
