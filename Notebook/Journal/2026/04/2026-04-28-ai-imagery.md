## Prompts

**Styled Flat Lay**

A carefully styled overhead flat lay on a [SURFACE: e.g. white marble / light oak / speckled cream stone], featuring [3-5 ITEMS: e.g. a linen-covered journal, a ceramic pen holder, a small succulent, a printed checklist, and a half-drunk cup of coffee], arranged with intentional breathing room -- not cramped. [BRAND COLOR] palette throughout. Even, diffused natural light from a side window. Clean editorial style, like a magazine shoot for your brand. The scene feels curated but lived in -- this is a workspace that means something.

SWAP IDEAS:
- Surface: dark moody marble / warm wood / linen fabric
- Color palette: dusty rose and cream / sage and white / black and gold
- Items: tarot cards and candle / fitness tracker and smoothie / laptop and headphones
