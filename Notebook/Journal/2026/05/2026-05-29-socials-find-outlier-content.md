# Socials -- Find Outlier Content

A prompt worth keeping for content planning.

**Prompt:** "find outlier content"

The idea is to use this to surface content that performs unusually well -- pieces that break the pattern in a good way. Useful for understanding what actually connects before building more of it.

[IG reel example](https://www.instagram.com/reel/DYzzQ3NxzMv/?igsh=MTdoeHU0dXBleHJ0MA==)
