## Why this matters

Build a batched, systematic content flow so Instagram has a vibe without demanding my whole life. Pinterest does the evergreen heavy lifting. There's a clear weekly rhythm I can actually follow without burning out. The end state: content goes out consistently because there's a system behind it, not willpower.
