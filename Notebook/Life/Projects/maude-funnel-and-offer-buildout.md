# Maude Funnel and Offer Buildout

**Part of:** [[offers]]

## Why this matters

Maude is an active coaching client running a paid-ads funnel into a low-priced masterclass that is meant to produce booked calls for her high-ticket Power Couple program. She is spending roughly 3000 dollars a month across retainer and ad budget, so the funnel has to become self-funding. The work spans ad measurement, cart conversion, pricing, page design, email sequencing and offer positioning.

## Status update

- 2026-07-15. Strategy call held, see [[2026-07-15-maude-funnel-and-ads-strategy-call]]. Cart conversion on the first offer page sits near 4 percent against a 15 to 20 percent target. Price is moving from 7 dollars to a 27 versus 37 split test. The ads agency is not tracking calls booked, which makes attribution impossible. Rise masterclass copy is being rewritten around three stacked outcomes.
