# Podcast Guesting

## What I think about here

Podcast guesting as a visibility, authority, referral, and relationship-building channel for Alyssa's business.

The durable idea: this should not be treated as random PR. It works best as a repeatable pipeline with positioning, show-fit criteria, personalized outreach, follow-up, interview prep, repurposing, and outcome tracking.
