# Offers

*[[business]] · [[promos]] · [[emails]] · [[back-of-house]] · [[clients]]*

---

**What this covers:** The products themselves — idea development, naming, positioning, pricing, course outlines, what's in the vault. Anything about *creating or defining* what I sell, including concept notes for future offers that don't have a file yet.

**What good looks like:** An offer suite that makes sense together — low-ticket products that lead somewhere, a flagship that anchors everything, and a handful of freebies that pre-qualify perfectly. Each offer has a clear reason to exist.

**What I am ignoring:** Promotional campaigns for those offers — that's [[promos]]. This is about the *offer itself*, not how I sell or deliver it.
