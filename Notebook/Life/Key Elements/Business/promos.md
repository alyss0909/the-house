# Promos

*[[business]] · [[emails]] · [[offers]] · [[socials]]*

---

**What this covers:** Everything that goes into a launch or campaign — promo calendar planning, launch emails, ads strategy, waitlist sequences, Black Friday and seasonal sale ideas, affiliate partnerships, webinar angles. The machinery of making a sale happen.

**What good looks like:** Easy repeatable launches, a seasonal rhythm with planned promo windows, solid launch emails, a freebie that pre-sells, and the whole thing documented so I can repeat it without reinventing it every time.

**What I am ignoring:** The emails in my regular weekly send (that's [[emails]]) and the offer details themselves (that's [[offers]]). [[promos]] is specifically the campaign window and its moving parts.
