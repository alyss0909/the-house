# Socials

*[[business]] · [[promos]] · [[emails]] · [[systematize-socials]]*

---

**What this covers:** Content for Instagram, Pinterest, and anything else living on a social platform — content frameworks, post copy, platform strategy, scheduling tools, ManyChat, carousel and story templates, and weekly content rhythm planning.

**What good looks like:** A batched, systematic content flow where Pinterest does the evergreen heavy lifting, Instagram has a vibe without demanding my whole life, and there's a clear weekly rhythm I can follow without burning out.

**What I am ignoring:** Paid ads (those go into [[promos]]). Social content that's directly part of a launch campaign probably wants a tag in [[promos]] too — this is my *organic* ongoing presence.
