Alyssa Content Production Relay
First, the real goal of your content
Your content should:
Turn your best thinking into repeatable belief shifts that make experienced business owners trust Back of House as the obvious next layer of support.
More specifically, content should do 6 jobs:
Make the right people feel found

“She understands the actual problem, not the Instagram version of the problem.”
Name the pattern they are living inside

content fatigue, over-marketing, lost ideas, no retrieval system, social as audition, brilliance leaking out through daily output.
Teach one useful thing

not a whole module, one sharp piece of the mechanism.
Create a gap

“I can see what is missing in my business now.”
Move them toward the right next action

comment keyword, freebie, tripwire, webinar, BOH.
Build the world of Back of House

email-first, archive-first, systems as relief, slow marketing, Pinterest as discovery, your best ideas as assets.
That’s the purpose. Not posting. Not engagement. Not content volume. Content is the public proof of your private operating system.
