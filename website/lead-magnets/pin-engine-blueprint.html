Questions

Before you grab it

Do I need to be technical to set this up? ▼
No. If you can copy-paste and open a terminal once, you can build this. The blueprint gives you both command files to copy word for word, and there is a Lite version that runs inside a regular Claude Project with zero installs.
Won't the AI just make up keywords? ▼
That is the exact failure this system is designed against. The commands require evidence behind every keyword: real search volume numbers, published trend data, or your own Pinterest search bar findings. Claude's job is analysis and judgment on real data, never invention.
Do I need PinClicks or any paid tool? ▼
No. The engine works with live web research alone. PinClicks (free trial available) is an optional upgrade that gives Claude real Pinterest search volume numbers to reason with, which sharpens the "can I actually win this keyword" judgment.
What do I still have to do myself? ▼
Two things: pick which researched keywords you like (a one-minute taste check in your tracker), and design the actual pin images. Research, judgment, and copywriting are handled. The creative call stays yours.
