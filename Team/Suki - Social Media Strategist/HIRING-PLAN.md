## THE MAKERS (the 6 functions)

### 1. THE IDEA PERSON — 🔴 TO HIRE (second)
- **Domain:** Comes up with the weekly post ideas — 10 simple promises you thumbs-up in a minute. Owns "what should we post." Stops at the idea; doesn't write hooks or copy.
- **Success:** You react to an idea with "obsessed" / "night and day." Ideas are simple promises a stranger instantly gets, each tied to the week's offer, none recycled from your old posts.
- **Trained by:** Suki, calibrated against your taste ledger (your real reacts) + the playbook.
- **Sources:** The playbook · your widened winners set · the current-IG swipe file · this week's theme + which offer it sells · the taste ledger.
- **Raise:** When ideas start landing "obsessed" first-try and Suki stops having to send any back.

### 2. THE HOOK PERSON — 🔴 TO HIRE (first)
- **Domain:** Writes the cover line — the scroll-stopper. Owns slide 1. Takes an approved idea, hands back hook options. Stops at the cover.
- **Success:** Covers carry a real payoff (fix/threat/confession/series/joke), 4–10 words, no reframe, pass the first-3-words and "so what?" tests. Measurably drive comments and follows.
- **Trained by:** Suki, calibrated on your hook forensics (your covers ranked by why they won) + the playbook.
- **Sources:** The playbook (Part 1) · the hook forensics · your widened winners set · the swipe file · the taste ledger.
- **Raise:** When a hook of theirs measurably drives sends/follows, and their covers stop getting rejected.

### 3. THE SUBSTANCE / ARC PERSON — 🟢 ALREADY FILLED
- **Domain:** Builds the middle — what each slide shows and the belief it shifts, with real persuasion and real numbers. The argument. Stops before final wording.
- **Success:** An arc the writer can build from with zero questions, and you approve first pass. Real numbers only (never a fake open rate), offer earned late.
- **Trained by:** Suki, calibrated on your slide-by-slide breakdowns + the arc rules.
- **Sources:** The playbook (Part 2) · your slide-by-slide forensics · the teaching source for that post (your BOH/offer file) · your real data for every number · the offer's meaning · the taste ledger.
- **Raise:** When their arcs stop needing your notes and the writer never has a question.
- **Note:** Filled, but built before the playbook existed and his first output was rejected. **Re-test him on the unstuck week under the new rules before we call him solid** — that re-test IS his evaluation.

### 4. THE WRITER — 🟢 ALREADY FILLED
- **Domain:** Writes the final deck in your voice, from the approved arc. Owns the words. Doesn't invent ideas, hooks, or the argument.
- **Success:** Decks that pass the "hide it among your real posts and nobody can spot the fake" test first try. You publish untouched.
- **Trained by:** Your voice judgment layer (your SOUL/STYLE/TASTE/anti-AI files) — the strongest-calibrated part of the whole system.
- **Sources:** The playbook · the approved arc · your voice judgment files · your real slides for that lane · the taste ledger.
- **Raise:** When you publish his decks with a light touch and never wrestle them.
- **Note:** Strong in his lane. The fix is keeping him IN it — he'd quietly been doing ideas + hooks too, which is exactly what hires #1 and #2 take off his plate.

### 5. THE RESEARCHER — 🟢 ALREADY FILLED (shared, not content-only)
- **Domain:** Goes and finds what's true right now — current IG craft, fresh swipe finds, competitor moves. Feeds the swipe file and the "will it perform" data.
- **Success:** Surfaces current, on-brand signals (not generic trends); refreshes the swipe file so hooks stay fresh; catches a platform shift before it costs you.
- **Trained by:** Existing role; Suki briefs him on what to look for.
- **Sources:** The trusted-source account list · the craft foundation · live platform-trend feeds.
- **Raise:** When he predicts a winner or a flop before it runs.

### 6. THE QUALITY-CHECK (the gate) — 🟡 NOT A HIRE — a gate Suki arms
- **Domain:** The mean critic every idea/hook/arc/deck passes before it reaches you. Catches slop so you don't have to.
- **Success:** Nothing reaches you that you'd reject; never flags your real work as fake.
- **Trained by:** Suki + you together — you said you want to arm this one *with* her.
- **Sources:** The playbook + the specific ammo per stage (the payoff rules for hooks, "is a number fake" for arcs, the anti-AI tells for decks).
- **Raise:** N/A — it's a gate, not a person. It gets sharper as we feed it your reacts.
