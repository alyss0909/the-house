**Bingeable Newsletters** — Essence: "Steal my repeatable structure that turns your messy thoughts into bingeable emails (fast)" — a reusable email-writing structure (5-Part Simmer Framework), not "content ideas." [PKM/My Life/Offers/bingeable-newsletters.md]
Teaches from: 2.3 The Simmer (Phase 2).

**Email List Revival Plan** — Essence: "The exact 5 re-engagement email strategies that are working now" — a 3-day plan to reheat an existing cold list, not list-growth advice. Belief: "your next buyer is already subscribed." [PKM/My Life/Offers/email-list-revival-plan.md]
Teaches from: 2.3 The Simmer; 1.4 The Double Yes Method.

**Cold Subscriber Automation** — Essence: an automation that keeps list hygiene "on repeat" without manual cleaning — deliverability maintenance, not a campaign. [PKM/My Life/Offers/cold-subscriber-automation.md]
Teaches from: 2.2 Not Grandad's Email Marketing (closest; no dedicated module).

**Pin Planner** — Essence: "Swipe the slow marketing strategy that turns 4 pieces of content into daily traffic, while you do literally anything else" — a Notion Pinterest calendar built on repurposing 4 existing pieces of content, not a Pinterest-growth-hacks guide. [PKM/My Life/Offers/pin-planner.md]
Teaches from: 3.1 Bread & Butter Content; 3.3 The Batch (Phase 3).

**Quarter Prompt** — Essence: "A quarterly planning prompt to set one focused intention for the next 90 days" — a single-prompt discipline tool (pick ONE thing), not a full planning system. [PKM/My Life/Offers/quarter-prompt.md]
Teaches from: 0.3 Cozy Business Plan; 0.1 Inspiration Pantry (The Prep Work).

**Soft CEO Sessions** — Essence: "Stay inspired all month with a collective of entrepreneurs doing LESS, better" — a live recurring group accountability/planning event, not a static resource. [PKM/My Life/Offers/soft-ceo-sessions.md]
Teaches from: 0.3 Cozy Business Plan.

**The Numbers** — Essence: "Know exactly how many people to grow your list by to hit your income goal" — a no-calculator profit-formula guide that reverse-engineers list/sales-call targets from an income goal, not general financial advice. [PKM/My Life/Offers/the-numbers.md]
Teaches from: 0.3 Cozy Business Plan (Cozy Biz Plan math).

**Biz Tracker** (status: needs_edits — included per LIVE-offer instructions since not retired/dead/idea) — Essence: "Exactly what we use to track, manage, and project the most important data in our online business" — an ongoing tracking/dashboard suite (sales, ops, marketing, growth + revenue projection), distinct from The Numbers' one-time formula. ## LANE 2 — Tripwire / Impulse ($9–$19)

**Unignorable Subject Lines** — Essence: "The best email in the world is useless if nobody clicks" — 80 tested subject lines by clicked-category, an open-rate tool, not general copywriting help. [PKM/My Life/Offers/unignorable-subject-lines.md]
Teaches from: 2.2 Not Grandad's Email Marketing.

**List Resuscitator** — Essence: "Big lists are cute, but engaged lists make money" — a done-for-you 3-day cold-subscriber revival campaign (paid version of Revival Plan freebie), not a list-growth product. [PKM/My Life/Offers/list-resuscitator.md]
Teaches from: 2.3 The Simmer; 1.4 The Double Yes Method.

**The 5 Minute Newsletter** — Essence: "The easiest email you'll ever send makes the most money" — a fill-in-the-blank weekly newsletter template/system that makes SENDING the bottleneck-fix, not an ideas-generator. [PKM/My Life/Offers/the-5-minute-newsletter.md]
Teaches from: 2.2 Not Grandad's Email Marketing; 2.3 The Simmer.

**90d Clock In** — Essence: "Turn your existing offers, leads, and ideas into finished assets, booked clients, and time-and-a-half paycheques" — a 90-day close-open-loops / one-department-at-a-time execution dashboard. It is a productivity/loop-closing tool, NOT generic "productivity content" — the core mechanic is finishing existing ideas one department at a time, with an Idea Pantry to park (not chase) new ones. [PKM/My Life/Offers/90d-clock-in.md]
Teaches from: 0.3 Cozy Business Plan; 0.1 Inspiration Pantry (The Prep Work).
