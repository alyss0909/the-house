# Calibration Answer Key

Set of 12, two formats. Randomized order was fixed at build. Class tally:
- **REAL (her published work, transcribed faithfully):** 4 — Items 02, 04, 07, 10
- **REAL-REJECT (covers/hooks she documented and rejected, expanded into competent plausible slide plans):** 2 — Items 05, 11
- **REAL-NEGATIVE (a real published piece SHE flagged as too-AI):** 1 — Item 08
- **FRESH DECOY (AI-written to pass a lazy rubric):** 5 — Items 01, 03, 06, 09, 12

A well-calibrated gate should end up KILLING or heavily EDITING items 01, 03, 05, 06, 08, 09, 11, 12 and PUBLISHING items 02, 04, 07, 10. If it publishes any decoy, the gate is too generous. If it kills a REAL item, the gate is trigger-happy and would reject Alyssa's own winners.

## Item 01 — FRESH DECOY (email, "smooth generic with lexicon sprinkled on")

Source: none — written for this test. Design: the median-competent AI marketing email. Clean, plausible, kind. Her surface lexicon dusted on top ("slow marketing method," "with intention," "🤎," "[FIRST NAME GOES HERE]").

**What should trip the gate:**
- Negative-parallelism reframe in the body: "Most creatives don't have a consistency problem, they have a system problem" — banned shape family ("It's not X, it's Y"), and it's the reader's belief being corrected in neutral prose (the AI register per ANTI-AI deep-read hypothesis).
- Dead opening / throat-clearing: "If you've ever built a beautiful content calendar in January and abandoned it by February, you're not alone."
- "shouldn't feel like a second full-time job," "free up the mental space," "show up with intention" — motivational-poster verbs, value-as-vague-noun, TASTE Disgusts.
- Zero detail that couldn't be invented. No jar count, no real Tuesday, no auction woman. Pure slot-fill.
- "Let's get into it" is one clause off "Let's dive in" (dead transition family).
- Neat resolution, no self-interruption, no aside. The TASTE "too AI" tell: label + generic description with no voice injected.

## Item 06 — FRESH DECOY (email, "over-corrected staccato")

Source: none — written for this test. Design: the documented over-correction failure mode — aggressively choppy, staccato, trying too hard at her rhythm. This is its own AI template (ANTI-AI: "Beware over-correction… aggressively choppy, adverb-free prose is its own AI template").

- Period density inverted: many hard periods, almost no trailing ".." dots — the exact signature of the flagged AI email (21 periods / 1 ellipsis).

- Subject "stop. writing. long. emails." is the over-corrected version of her staccato — reads as A/B-tested-into-blandness (fails the wrongness test; nothing is weird/coined, just clipped).
- No warmth, no aside, no real detail. It performs her rhythm without her substance — voice without substance, FABLE failure mode #4.

## Design notes for the calibration author (meta)

- **The two REAL-REJECTs (05, 11) are the calibration crown jewels:** both have competent slide plans on purpose. A lazy gate that judges "is the deck good?" will publish them. A calibrated gate that judges the COVER against ANTI-AI (05 = recycled winner skeleton / imitation tell; 11 = reframe-on-a-cover) kills them. These two most sharply separate a real gate from a rubric-passer.
- **Item 08 (REAL-NEGATIVE)** tests whether the gate matches Alyssa's OWN eye — it's genuinely her published work that she rejected. Killing/editing it = calibrated. Publishing it = missing her standard.
- **Item 12 (fact-gap decoy)** tests whether the gate is a trust gate or just a voice gate. Its voice is fine; its facts are invented. Only a gate that checks provenance catches it.
- **Items 06 (over-corrected) and 01 (smooth generic)** are the two documented AI-tell templates — opposite failure modes (too-choppy vs. too-smooth). A good gate catches both; a gate tuned for only one will miss the other.
- **Difficulty ladder (easiest to hardest to catch as fake):** 06 (obvious staccato) < 01 (generic) < 03 / 09 (clean carousels w/ one fabricated or one rule-of-three tell) < 12 (great voice, invented facts) < 05 / 11 (competent decks, only the cover is wrong) ≈ 08 (genuinely hers, but she rejected it).
