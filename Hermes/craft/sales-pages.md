## Page skeleton (BOH pattern — the flagship template)

1. **Pain-state open, no personal narrative** — positioning line + "is the current vibe..👀" style reflection: "Your business has become way too needy... There's no breathing room, no built-in momentum."
2. **Structural refrain** — one diagnosis line repeated 3+ times through the page: "It's all very high maintenance... Your business model is too high maintenance." Understatement after weight.
3. **Validation before dismantle** — "And it's not because your offer isn't good enough. Or because you're not posting enough."
4. **Contrarian authority move** — "I DID SOMETHING WAY MORE USEFUL. I looked back at what always worked in online business." Researcher, never guru.
5. **Origin story as proof** — Portugal/baby beat, compressed: "Once I had a real baby, it's like a veil was lifted and I suddenly realized my business was needier than my newborn."
6. **System reveal with metaphor-named sections** — restaurant names ARE the architecture: Mise en Place, The House Specials, Warm Plate Method, Traffic Pantry, Soft Sales System. "Prep once, serve again and again."
7. **Two-scene contrast mic-drop** — equal-length with/without paragraphs (the dirty-dishes passage).
8. **Offer reveal via mirrored pivot** — "so what if instead of hiring one person YOU HAD AN ENTIRE DEPARTMENT working for you" → CTA.
9. **Value stack** with individual dollar values; bots framed as "an entire team."
10. **Testimonials in three waves** — early single quote, mid-page depth block, late converting quote. First-name only ("- Rosey"), never editorialized, never sales-page-parroting.
11. **FAQ near the end ("inquiring minds...")** — the most clinical, metaphor-dropped register on the page. "I won't lie" candor. Refund policy addressed head-on ("Unfortunately, that means we don't offer refunds").
12. **CTAs escalate**: task → emotional → permission. Vary the phrasing; ALL CAPS = shout, mixed case = quiet ask. Catalog register: "JOIN BACK OF HOUSE NOW >>", "SOUNDS DELISH >>", "I'll have what she's having", "DO THE MATH FOR ME! >>".

## Sell the Hawaii, not the flight

Her own rule, from the module that teaches sales pages: "people do not buy a flight. They buy a destination... The biggest mistake I see is people put on their sales page so many details about the damn flight." Feature lists come AFTER the destination is fully painted. And the disruption check before writing at all: "If your philosophy is not disruptive... nothing else on this page will land. Is this bacon and butter, or am I giving oatmeal?"

## Wordsmithing rules (from her teaching + pages)

- Use the reader's exact words ("what are they Googling at 2:00 AM").
- Go one layer deeper on pain: "cancelling your kid's sports camp," not "inconsistent revenue."
- Frame as "wouldn't it be nice," never "you're doing it wrong."
- Marquee text = standalone billboard declarations ("HUSTLE CULTURE IS OVERCOOKED"); headers make arguments.
- Life-vision lists use human-scale images: "Read poetry by the window," "Call your grandma" — never "more freedom."
- Urgency = real spots/windows only, coupon codes framed casually ("use code ORDERUP... before friday").

## Register calibration by product tier

- Flagship (BOH-world): full restaurant metaphor, long page, all 12 beats.
- Low-ticket/tool pages: metaphor drops to accent-level or zero (Biz Tracker, CEO Year pages are feature-forward) — do NOT force the food thing onto a $19 Notion template.
- Discount framing: "Regularly $X, but today, it's just $Y."
- ⚠ Do not imitate the stale-voice pages (The Numbers, Daily Profit Activities, TPE) — pre-soft-CEO era.

## Deep-read additions (2026-07-06, from the flagship BOH page itself)

First quote-level pass on the canonical page (full study: [[Hermes/learning/deep-reads/2026-07-06-0410-boh-sales-page]]). Beyond the 12 beats:

- **Coined-term architecture**: invent the audience name, then build furniture on it — "Designed for the time connoisseur*" carries a real asterisk-footnote ("a CEO who knows the value of an intentional pace."), repeats 3x as a refrain CTA block, AND gets its own FAQ entry. Coin → footnote → refrain → FAQ.
- **Reader-voice objection headers mid-page**, distinct from the FAQ: "wait not another "system" alyssa" (lowercase, addresses her by name) and "So I'll never post on social? Won't that look bad?" — objection voiced AS the reader, answered in her warmest rant register ("I have to set the record straight!!!"). FAQ stays the clinical version.
- **Commiserate-first FAQ answers**: the follow-through-fear question (her audience's #1 purchase barrier) opens with one word — "Same." — before the fix. Admit you share the fear, then solve it.
- **No dead zones**: legal/payment/geo boilerplate gets full voice — anti-piracy warning flips into "going in with a friend is a biz-bestie-love-language" + real course-share policy + "(and don't worry we won't judge your netflix accts)"; "🍁 Canadian Cuties, we've got you."
- **🤎 TLDR block closes every curriculum phase** — the section-level skim path. Steps describe the flight; the TLDR sells the Hawaii ("you'll have a lean offer suite that pre-sells itself").
- **Comparison lists use the SAME ✅ on both sides** — no ❌-vs-✅ theater; sentence content carries the contrast. Emoji-bulleted feeling lists (🥱📉🕳️ pains → 🛎️🧸☁️ wants) are the textured variant.
- **Emoji doing grammar, one per zone**: "Pinterest drives the traffic 🤝 Email does the converting." (🤝 = conjunction); a single mid-prose 👏 at the origin story's decision beat.
- **Personified channel proof**: email is "she... (aka THAT girl)" in the FAQ's data answer — warm, once.
- **Value stack stays line-item lumpy** ($40/$50/$100/$150/$500 per item) — never rounded into one tidy bundle number.
- Resolved 2026-07-07 (see ANTI-AI.md "Resolved by precedent" + STYLE §6): the ORDERUP timer, "quietly" 2x, and "JOIN...NOW >>" command CTAs are no longer open flags — all three closed using existing precedent/evidence.
