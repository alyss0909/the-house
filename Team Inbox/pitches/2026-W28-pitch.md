### WED-B — How To Get Found On Pinterest Without Posting Every Day

**Cover:** How To Get Found On Pinterest Without Posting Every Day

1. Cover: title + hook
2. Introduce the two types of visibility: feed-based (Instagram, reach expires daily) vs index-based (Pinterest, content is stored and resurfaced)
3. Proof, stacked with real Pinterest stats: 38% of viral pins are over two years old; pins accumulate instead of expiring; one page can become 200 pins and ~73k visitors a year
4. Step 1: search your topic in the Pinterest search bar; the suggestions that pop up ARE your keywords
5. Step 2: put those keywords everywhere: profile, board titles, pin titles, descriptions (naturally, don't stuff)
6. Step 3: keep making new images: a fresh pin is a new image + new title on the same link, Pinterest reads it as brand new
7. CTA: comment Pinterest → Pin Planner

Hooks: "How To Get Found On Pinterest Without Posting Every Day" · "Get Found In Search While You're Offline" · "Your Content Deserves A Longer Shelf Life"
Sources: Slowly Viral Class (feed vs index; 38% stat; 200-pin / 73k math; fresh-pin rule) · 3.3 The Batch (keyword planner; board setup)
