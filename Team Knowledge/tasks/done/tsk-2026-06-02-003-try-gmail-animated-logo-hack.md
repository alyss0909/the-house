# Try the Gmail animated logo hack (Gemini → GIF → sender icon)

## What this is

A quick-win email marketing differentiator. Almost nobody has a moving sender icon in Gmail — doing it makes your emails stand out in inboxes before they're even opened.

## Steps

1. Drag your business logo into Gemini → prompt: "animate my logo" (ask for minor movement, not wild creative license)
2. Download the video → convert to GIF via an mp4-to-gif converter (search "mp4 to gif", use the top result)
3. Go to your Google account → click the camera icon on your profile
4. Upload the GIF as your profile photo
