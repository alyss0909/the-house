# IDX platform research — evaluate display quality and responsiveness

## What this is

Continue research on IDX (Internet Data Exchange) providers for embedding real estate listings into the Editor in Residence website on Squarespace.

Andrew confirmed the technical side is fine — any provider that delivers an iframe/JavaScript embed code will work with Squarespace. The decision is editorial and aesthetic.

## What to evaluate

- **Display quality** — does it look clean and polished, or does it look like Craigslist circa 2004?
- **Responsiveness** — resize the browser across breakpoints; does the embed reflow properly or break?
- **Custom styling options** — can the embed colors, fonts, or layout be adjusted to match the site?
- **IDX-specific features** — any features Jillian specifically needs (saved searches, lead capture, map views, etc.)

## Done when

- Shortlist of providers is ready with display/responsiveness notes.
- Andrew is notified and ready to trial the embed code.
