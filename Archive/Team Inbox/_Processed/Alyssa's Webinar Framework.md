# MY WEBINAR FRAMEWORK

  

THE OPEN

There are 10 main components to the open. Keep this REALLY simple and brief. You’ll want to move them through the opening slides in about 10 minutes. 

  
  

TITLE SLIDE 

Add your sexy title here. If you don’t have a subtitle yet, make sure you add this now. Remember to keep the focus on Hawaii.

  

WHO IT’S FOR 

Hype them up and call them forward. What type of person is this for? What’s the outcome they want to achieve?

  

This webinar is for [this type of person] who wants to achieve this [outcome]. 

  

WHAT YOU’RE COVERING & WHAT THEY GET 

What are they getting from this webinar?  WHY should they stick around? Fill them in on the juicy details and benefits -  and remember to keep this really clear and simple. 

In the next 45 minutes I’m gonna cover how you can get ___ without ever doing ___. 

  

YOUR PROMISE TO THEM

By the end of this webinar you will... [one sentence that makes a promise]. This should be related to the "one thing" of your webby, and closely relate your registration page bullets.

  

THE HOUSEKEEPING

What basic information do they need to know about the webinar experience before you dive in? This is where to give them the rundown on how to comment, ask questions, navigate the tech, get the workbook etc.

  

“Okay, now before we get into the goods, let me just check in…”

  

CALL THEM OUT 

Gently, of course. ;) In this section you want to highlight that you understand they’re current struggle. What’s the price of them staying where they are? What’s the actual problem they’re experiencing? Give a few examples. The more specific you are, the better.“These are just symptoms of a bigger problem.”

  

THE ‘REAL’ PROBLEM

Use this slide to reframe the way they’re thinking about their current problems by introducing “the shifts.”

  

“If the last slide resonates, then you’re in good hands. The problems you’re having are the symptoms of a bigger problem. The real problem is that you haven’t made the “shifts.” Once you make them…”

  

FUTURE PACE

What happens when they make the shifts? What will change in their life? (add 2-4 bullets here) “once you make these shifts you will…..HAWAII HAWAII”

  

“Before we go forward, let me introduce myself.”

  

FLEX YOUR CREDIBILITY 

What’s your specialty? What results have you gotten for people that relate to what your ideal client wants? Keep this short and sweet.

  
  

HOW YOU DISCOVERED “THE SHIFTS”

How did you discover what you’re about to teach them? Tell your story from the perspective of how it can help them. Keep this SHORT AND SWEET - one min max, this webby is not about you, it's about them - allow them to see themselves in your story of discovering the shifts, what NIGHTMARES were you experiencing before that they might be experiencing now? And what DREAM results did you have once you discovered these shifts? Let them picture themselves living that Hawaiian dream hehe.

  
“There are [number] key shifts you must make to [get this hawaii outcome].  I’m gonna walk you through all 5 of them right now.”

  
  

Remember you should cover all that in 10 mins or less! (This should be about 15-20 slides) 

  
  

THE SHIFTS:

By now you already have the shift purpose and the disruptive title. Now it's time to deliver in each one of these shifts. 

  

Remember, this is more about getting them to have a perspective shift than it is about actually telling them everything they need to know about this topic. If you notice yourself TEACHING too much try coming back to showing them WHAT they need to do, and WHY it's so essential, without spending too much time on the HOW (generally you cover the HOW in your coaching / course). Focus on what they can be/do/have if they take action here.

  
  

CASE STUDIES

Pair each shift with a case study that best demonstrates the application of the shift you’re sharing. If you don’t have case studies yet, share examples of how YOU applied the shifts and the types of results you created as a result OR share examples of how public figures, famous entrepreneurs, historical institutions etc used this model in real life. 

  

Remember these are NOT testimonials for your program, they are PROOF that what you just taught can work with REAL people.

  
  

SHIFT BEST PRACTICES:

- shift 1 = shift their belief around the problem (you THINK your problem is this but it’s really THIS…)
    
- shift 2 = share the solution 
    
- shift 3 = share one fringe benefit of implementing this solution, not only will you have THIS cool outcome you want but you'll also have THIS that you also want. 
    

Whenever possible get them thinking: THIS is the old way, THIS is why the old way is total BS, and HERE is the new way (your way), and why it's superior! 

  
  

THE CLOSE

The Close has 5 key components. Remember, your pitch is absolutely essential to getting them the outcome they want so CONFIDENCE IS KEY. Keep an eye out for apologetic language (that’s not the vibe, and it’s not helping anyone). At this point in the webinar, your offer is the logical next solution that they’ll be looking for. 

  
  

THE PROMISE

Start these slides with “Today I promised…” and recap your promise to them from the open. The purpose of this is to remind them of the reason they came to the webinar and to prove (in the next slide) that you delivered on all of those promises.

  

THE RECAP

For this section, quickly recap the shifts you’ve taken them through. End with something like, “these 5 shifts alone are enough to [OUTCOME THEY WANT HERE].

  

NOW YOU HAVE A CHOICE

At this point, you’re going to direct them toward two paths….what they can do themselves with the tools and advice you’ve just given them OR paint the picture for them of what even better outcome they can get if…

  

“You can DIY this using the shifts I’ve just shown you OR you can [OUTCOME HERE] and not only that, but [MORE OUTCOME HERE]. If you choose option 2, here’s what I’ve got for you…”

  

HOW I CAN HELP & THE PITCH

Now it's time to pitch baby! 

Depending on how you’re selling your offer, choose one of the paths below. Depending on the path you take, use and modify the slides from the template.

  

If you’re booking calls from the webinar, this next step is nice and simple: all you need to do is show them how to book a call with you, let them know why you take these free calls, and demonstrate what they will get out of it. Make sure to future pace them here about what it looks like to book a call, what happens when they click the link etc. 

  

If you’re pitching from the webinar, now it’s time to introduce your program and get into your sales pitch. 

  

Here are a few important things to include 

  

- The main outcome of Your Offer  - this should be related to your webinar 
    
- How it works & what’s included - give them the quick breakdown of the program walking them through your phases and deliverables etc, remember to keep coming back to what they will BE DO OR HAVE by the end of each phase
    
- The program bonuses - make it sound sexy and don't forget to include your urgency bonus that’s only available DURING the webinar (I like to lead with this bonus) 
    
- Testimonials - add in some sexy screenshots or testimonials here to do the social selling FOR YOU. 
    
- Multiple calls to action - every couple of slides add in a call to action to let them know where to click, what URL to go to or what their payment options are, then keep selling
    
- Future Pace - Explain what happens after they buy, what will they get AS SOON AS they join? When is your next call? What can they get started on ASAP? This is really exciting and enticing so don't forget about it.
