# The Content OS (your riff, made real)

System wiring needs your yes before I build it (your rule). Here's the design. Everything below runs on the rebuilt Hermes brain + the new forensics layer.

## The goal of your content (answered from your own course)

You asked. Your course already answers it, in one sentence from the Slowly Viral class:

> "Pinterest drives traffic to your best evergreen content, which pre-qualifies visitors and sends them to your email list, where you consistently sell your signature offer."

And per email: **"Every email you send should do 3 things: Grow, Nourish & Offer"** (0.2 Sign Off Specials). Per pin: **"Every pin we put out is funnel-forward"** (3.3 The Batch). So the Content OS optimizes for exactly one thing: **qualified email list growth toward the signature dish** - saves/shares/follows are instruments, never the goal. Every piece must name its funnel job before it's written.

## The slide-breakdown + hooks + arcs layer (built today, from your real decks)

- **Hook rules**: 4-10 words, payoff mandatory (fix/threat/confession/series/joke), never a reframe on a cover, no "you" on covers, period never question mark, eyebrow = series-with-stake or audience qualifier. From your 12 ranked covers ([carousel-hook-forensics](../PKM/Second%20Brain/analysis/carousel-hook-forensics.md)).
- **Slide roles**: cover → credibility/audience-mirror → one-point-per-slide teaching (bold claim + "My advice:" tier) → emotional-validation hinge (mid-third, no CTA, "earns the right to offer") → pattern interrupt at ~75-80% (never last) → comment-word CTA. Archetype variants documented (Trend Report, Data Argument, Manifesto, Step Framework, Product Reveal, Story/POV).
- **Concept arcs**: your decks already run AIDA (product reveals), PAS-with-relief-pivot, PASTOR/villain (Organic Marketing Is Dying), BAB-manifesto (Wasting Time). The strategist pass names the arc up front; the through-line is fixed: ≥3 problem slides before any solution, offer earned after 60% of the deck.
- **First comment**: your real mechanic is the comment-keyword in caption; first comment stays an optional personality beat, not SEO stuffing.

## Weekly themes

Your captions data carries the slots (Week 1 Email, Monday edu, Wednesday cut, For The Girls Friday). The strategist pass holds a theme calendar: **Email · Pinterest · Productivity · Systems** rotating, FTG every Friday. You approve the calendar monthly; the relay fills it.

## Your "gold standard" question - yes, and here's the mechanism

"Maybe it's my job to gold standard one of each?" Exactly right, and it's the highest-leverage 30 minutes you can give this. One approved exemplar per lane (edu carousel, offer carousel, FTG, Simmer, Soft Sunday, sales email, pin) goes into `Hermes/examples/good/` as THE benchmark for its lane. Everything generated gets lineup-tested against your gold standard, not against a rubric. (This is also the Blotato brand-brief pattern, but grounded in real pieces instead of a questionnaire.)
