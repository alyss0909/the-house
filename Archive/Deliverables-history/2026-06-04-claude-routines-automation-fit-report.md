## Executive Summary

The strongest business fit is not to copy the routines one by one. The useful move is to merge the best ideas into a few Alyssa-specific operating routines.

After Alyssa's 2026-06-04 follow-up, the revised shortlist is:

1. **Weekly Vault Formatting and Drift Sweep** - centered on routine 21, adapted away from code docs and toward myPKA formatting, wikilinks, related notes, related tasks, and vault interconnection hygiene.
2. **Personal Knowledge Base Opportunity Digest** - centered on routine 27, close to Semantic Recurrence but more explicit about "could this become a blog post, project, decision, offer, or conversation?"
3. **Weekly Status and Pattern Report** - centered on routine 09, reading daily notes and task files from the past 7 days to report completed work, in-progress work, blockers, leftovers, and recurring patterns.
4. **Listening and Research Aggregator** - merges competitor monitoring, social listening, audience listening, news, YouTube/video inputs, and weekly research aggregation into one signal-finding routine.
5. **Video Watcher** - an added skill idea from Alyssa, not from the Notion page. It watches or processes videos/transcripts, extracts useful ideas, claims, frameworks, examples, and content angles, then feeds the Listening and Research Aggregator or Second Brain.

This matches the actual open work: [[tsk-2026-06-01-002-second-brain]], [[tsk-2026-06-01-003-semantic-recurrence-layer]], [[systematize-socials]], [[rush-order]], [[back-of-house]], Blog & Pin, monthly workshops, and the current capture/automation layer around Telegram and Meetily.

## Methodology

Pax inspected the public Notion parent page and all 29 linked child routine pages. Each routine was scored against:

- current open tasks in `Team Knowledge/tasks/INDEX.md`
- active projects and goals in `PKM/My Life/Projects/` and `PKM/My Life/Goals/`
- active offers in `PKM/My Life/Offers/`
- the Second Brain build plan and copy routing system
- setup friction, especially whether the routine requires new connectors, webhooks, SaaS accounts, or ongoing manual care

Confidence is **Medium-High**. The source pages were directly read, and the local business context was read from myPKA. The limitation is that the routine prompts are generic examples, not tested against Alyssa's actual tools.

## Pax Recommendation

Start with one pilot, not four. After Alyssa's follow-up, Pax's revised first-pilot recommendation is **Weekly Vault Formatting and Drift Sweep** because it has clear boundaries, protects the vault immediately, and can run on local markdown without waiting for connectors.

Second pilot: **Personal Knowledge Base Opportunity Digest**, because it connects Semantic Recurrence to concrete outputs like blog posts, decisions, offer ideas, and content angles.

Third pilot: **Weekly Status and Pattern Report**, because it helps Alyssa see what is left over, what is blocking, and what keeps repeating.

The **Listening and Research Aggregator** is high-value, but it needs a source list before implementation. That one should be designed after Alyssa names the first recurring inputs.

Add **Video Watcher** as a manual Pax skill before automating it. This is low-friction and immediately useful: Alyssa can drop a video link or transcript and ask Pax to extract ideas, claims, frameworks, content angles, and what should be saved into the Second Brain.
