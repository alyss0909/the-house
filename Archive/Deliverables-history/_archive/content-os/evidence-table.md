## Solution Principle

When an exact slot example is missing, do not ask Alyssa to solve the blank first. Propose three grounded options:

1. A conservative version based on the closest existing post type.
2. A braver version based on a cross-format source like a Soft Sunday, Simmer, or webinar.
3. A For The Girls version based on emotional lane, not business theme.

Then Alyssa reacts to concepts, not empty gaps.
