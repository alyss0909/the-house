# Vault is Ground Truth, Not Session Logs

Weekly wrap generators and open-thread audits must verify vault state directly. They must not trust session log narrative.

Session logs say what was intended. The vault shows what exists.

The W23 weekly wrap incorrectly marked "Second BOH webinar" as done with "one still missing" because the generator read session narrative rather than checking the vault. The vault had all three webinars. The narrative was simply behind the work.

## The two-condition rule

To close a thread, both conditions must be true:

1. The session log says it was done.
2. The vault artifact exists at the expected path.

Either condition alone is insufficient. A session log entry with no artifact means the work may not have landed. A vault artifact with no session log entry means the log is incomplete. Both must confirm before a thread closes.
