# Blog Title Generator
*M3.1 · P3: Traffic Pantry*

---

> [!abstract] Quick Read
> **What's here:** 50+ content title formulas organized across 6 style categories, each with a distinct emotional hook.
> **Key insight:** The category you choose signals the tone of the piece before a word is written — Hot Take for disruption, Proof for credibility, BTS for intimacy. Picking the category IS part of the creative brief.
> **Surfaces in:** Starting any blog post, email, or social content; testing multiple angles on the same topic; briefing a content writer on format and tone; teaching content strategy in workshops or coaching.
> **Links in:** [[Library/Programs/back-of-house/3. Traffic Pantry/INDEX|P3: Traffic Pantry]]

---

## Input Variables

Fill these in before running a formula. The bracketed placeholders below map to these inputs.

| Variable | What it means |
|---|---|
| Pain Point | e.g. burnout, feeling invisible, posting with no results |
| Desired Result | e.g. fully booked calendar, viral reels |
| Audience | e.g. burned-out coaches, freelance designers |
| Action / Transformation | e.g. grow your email list, get more consult calls |
| Relatable Identity | e.g. a tired AF entrepreneur, an anxious creative |
| Tactic / Strategy | e.g. low-effort visibility, DMs that convert |
| Belief / Myth / Rule | e.g. "more content = more sales" |
| Adjective 1 | e.g. lazy, weird, rebellious, anti-hustle |
| Adjective 2 | e.g. messy, spicy, bite-sized, chaotic |
| Number | e.g. 3, 5, 7, 10 |
| Subject Matter | e.g. content strategy, your sales process |
| Program Name | e.g. The Unburnout Method |
| Pop Culture Reference | e.g. Rihanna, Taylor Swift, Succession |

## 🌶️ Hot Take

*Disruptive, contrarian, counter-intuitive — challenges what the audience thinks they know.*

- Why [Common Advice] Is Secretly Making [Frustrating Problem] Worse
- Stop [Popular Behavior] If You Actually Want [Big Desire]
- If You Believe [Innocent-Sounding Belief] You're Probably [Painful Consequence]
- You're Not [Struggle Identity] You're Just Doing [Wrong Strategy]
- The Truth About [Popular Belief] That No One in [Industry] Wants to Admit
- [Number] Myths You Should Know About [Topic]
- [Number] Things You Need to Know About [Topic] Before You [Action]
- [Number] Experts Reveal How to [Achieve Result]
- [Number] Things You Should Never [Do / Believe / Follow] If You Want [Result]

---

## 🎓 How To

*Educational, empowering — removes the obstacle and shows the path.*

- How to [Big Win] Without [Overwhelming Thing Everyone Hates]
- The [Lazy/Feral/Secret] Way I [Achieved X] in [Tiny Timeframe]
- How to [Get X] Even If You [Relatable Struggle or Identity]
- Exactly What to Do When [Crisis or Triggering Moment] So You Don't [Worst Case Outcome]
- The [Number]-Step Process I Use to [Achieve Smart Result]
- How to Create [Thing] That Gets [Outcome]
- How to Tell If [Situation or Symptom] Is Actually [Deeper Issue]
- How to [Outcome] When You Feel [Relatable Emotion or State]
- How to Build a [Thing] You Can Be Proud Of
- How to Turn [Problem] Into [Powerful Outcome]
- [Number] Things to Do Before You [Big Action]

---

## 🌈 Bridge Builder

*Bridging where the reader is to where they want to be — practical, structured, hopeful.*

- 3 Simple Tactics to [Achieve X] Without Burning Out
- The 3-Part Process I Teach Clients to [Get Desired Result]
- 3 Strategies That Will Instantly Improve Your [Area of Life/Business]
- The 3 Things That Changed Everything for My [Client Avatar]
- My 3 Favorite Tools for [Solving Specific Problem]
- [Number]-Point Checklist for [Big Result or Transformation]
- [Number] Essential Things to Know Before You [Action That Relates to Offer]
- [Number] Steps to Starting a Hugely Successful [Thing]

---

## 🧾 Proof

*Social proof, case studies, data — builds credibility through evidence.*

- How [Client Type] Achieved [Impressive Result] Using Just [Number] Key Shifts
- The Data Behind Why [Controversial Belief You Hold] Actually Works
- What [Pop Culture Character or Celebrity] Can Teach Us About [Your Topic]
- I Used This Method to Help [Client Avatar] Go From [Pain Point] to [Result]
- [Number] Real Client Wins That Prove [Big Idea or Philosophy] Isn't Just Theory
- [Number] Proven Methods for Accelerating [Result or Outcome]
- [Number] Questions Answered About [Your Method / Niche]
- [Number] Case Studies That Show How [Method] Creates [Transformation]
- Tutorial: [Name of Your Signature Process or Offer]

---

## 📝 Listicle

*Skimmable, high-value, shareable — works across SEO, social, and email.*

- [Number] Things I Do in My Business That Would Horrify Most "Experts"
- [Number] Weird Systems That Keep Me Sane Paid and Slightly Unhinged
- [Number] Harsh Truths I Wish More People Knew About [Your Industry]
- [Number] Insider Secrets to Achieving [Desirable Result]
- [Number] Free Ways to [Get a Result] Without [Painful Tradeoff]
- [Number] Lesser-Known Tools for [Doing Something Better or Easier]
- [Number] Things You Can Do With [Unusual Tool or Concept]
- The Ultimate Guide to [Topic] for [Specific Person/Stage]
- [Number] Signs You're Ready to [Transformation] Even If You Don't Feel Like It Yet
- The Beginner's Guide to [X] That Doesn't Suck or Patronize You
- Exactly What I'd Do If I Were Starting [Topic/Business/Goal] from Scratch
- [Number] Mistakes I Made When I Tried to [Result] and What I'd Do Differently
- [Number] Tools I Swear By for [Result] Without [Painful Thing]
- The Real Reason You're Not [Achieving Result] (And It's Not What You Think)
- What No One Tells You About [Process/Path/Transformation]
- [Number] Underrated Ways to [Achieve X] That I Wish I'd Tried Sooner
- I Did [Wild Tactic] for [Timeframe] and Here's What Actually Happened

## 🕯️ BTS

*Behind the scenes, confessional, intimate — builds trust through vulnerability and realness.*

- Confession: I Used to [Cringe Habit] Until I [Reframe or Strategy]
- Here's What I Actually Do All Day as a [Job Title]
- The Dumbest Thing I Believed About [Topic] and What Finally Rewired It
- I Built [Result] Without Ever Doing [Common Industry Tactic]
- [Number] Client Red Flags I Ignore and the One That ALWAYS Bites Me in the Ass
- I Let You In On [Cringe/BTS/Relatable Topic] and Now I'm Booked Out
