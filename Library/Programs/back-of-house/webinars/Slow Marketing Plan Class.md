## [00:57:25] The Five Slow Marketing Rules

1. **Create once, let it work forever.** Anything you create, you should be able to create once and then let it work forever. If you wanna replace nonstop posting with content that's done once, it needs to follow this.
2. **Divest from posting daily.** Stop normalizing feeling like you have to post in order to stay relevant.
3. **Focus on creating things that are impactful rather than excessive.** Going deeper with your audience rather than trying to find a bigger audience.
4. **Use evergreen content to really build trust.** Those four bread and butter pieces of content are meant to be so good that you are happy for as many people as possible to see them.
5. **Automate thoughtfully.** Not everything. How do I actually just replace some of the stuff that I am actively doing every single day, and can I replace it in a way that's still just as effective as me doing it?
