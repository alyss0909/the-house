## Phase Two: Plan

### The Source

Time to get in front of your dream clients and find out what they NEED. You can go live with a couple titles ready and ask ppl 1-10 how exciting is this for you and why or why not? Or go to other ppls fb groups where you think your ideal client is, or use IG stories and use the meter, or if you have an audience you can ask "what's ONE thing you want me to make free content on, leave it below."

Brainstorm 4 titles you can pitch to your audience below:

### The Fire

Now that you've seen their responses — Which recurring topic will you choose? Are you passionate about this topic?

### The Outcome

What is the main OUTCOME of your freebie?

What's the quick win your dream client will experience when they opt in? (** don't skip this!)

Can you get more specific with this outcome? Is it clear?

### The Process

What is your process or method for achieving this outcome? How did you do what you're going to help your dream client do with this freebie? How did YOU get this outcome? Or how do you help your clients achieve this outcome? Reverse engineer your process below:

How can you make it step by step so that they get results quickly? 3-7 steps max. Write the steps of your process down below.

TAKE ACTION: what ONE action can you get your dream client to take after each step above?

OPTIONAL: add a CASE STUDY to each step, for inspo, credibility, and more importantly to prove your point. This can be a case study from you, a client, or really any stat from the industry.

> 🛎️ If you're feeling stuck on how to structure your freebie, don't spiral. Just grab The Framework. It's what we use to map out what I call "The Perfect PDF" and quizzes without the mental gymnastics.

### The Sexy Title

Here are two sexy title formulas to help you create something buzz-worthy. Go back to the 4 ideas you wrote in "the source" and re-work them using this formula to create a sexy title:

**Formula A:**
`___desire____ in ____amount of time____ even if ____reason they can't___`

Ie: map out a year's worth of content in a day even if you're the queen of procrastination

**Formula B:**
`___ Number + adjective + strategy____to help you ____ desire 1 & desire 2___`

Ie: 3 simple productivity hacks to help you work 4 days a week while making 10k months

## Opt-In Page Bullets

**USE THESE QUESTIONS TO BRAINSTORM YOUR OPT IN PAGE BULLETS:**

- What are 3 cool things they'll learn inside?
- What 2 outcomes will they have by the end?
- What problem are you solving?
- Will they transform something just by using your freebie?
- What can they be/do/have if they take action here?
- What negative outcome / feeling can they avoid if they take action?
- What are they insecure about? Why are they thinking they CAN'T actually have that outcome? Reassure them here.
