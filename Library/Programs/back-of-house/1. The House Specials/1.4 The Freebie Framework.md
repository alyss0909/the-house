# The Freebie Framework
*M1.4 · P1: The House Specials*

> [!abstract] Quick Read
> **What's here:** DFY structural frameworks for two freebie formats — PDF guide and Quiz. The fill-in skeleton for building either from scratch.
> **Key insight:** Two proven formats: the PDF guide (3-thing structure with what/why/action) and the quiz (4-result mapping with strategic result blurbs). Both end with a CTA toward the tripwire or main offer.
> **Surfaces in:** When building a new freebie and needing the architecture; when a student is setting up a quiz opt-in for the first time.

## PDF Freebie Framework

1. **Cover Page** — designed
2. **About You** (½ page) — "that's really about them"
3. **How This Works** (½ page) — position as authority before teaching anything
4. **Thing 1**
   - What is it?
   - Why is it important?
   - How to take action?
5. **Thing 2** — same structure
6. **Thing 3** — same structure
7. **Bonus points:** Go back and add case studies or proof to each Thing (or as many as you can)
8. **Congratulations Page** — next steps and CTA → lead to tripwire, book a call, or main offer

## Quiz Freebie Framework

**Recommended tool:** [Tally](https://tally.so/) *(updated recommendation)*

**Structure:**

1. **Define 4 Results upfront** — Result 1 / Result 2 / Result 3 / Result 4
2. **How This Works** — brief framing before questions begin
3. **Questions 1–5** — each answer maps to one of the 4 results
4. **How to Add Up Results** — scoring instructions
5. **Result Blurbs (1–4)** — *"Be strategic here — use these answers to get them to see the gap between where they are and where you know they wanna go. This is your chance to inject lots of 'omg that's SO me' moments. They'll see you as the expert when you demonstrate you've seen their kind of case before. Feel free to hint at good solutions now too."*
6. **Congratulations Page** — next steps and CTA
