## The Tripwire Product Checklist

Use this after you've decided on a product idea to test if it's genuinely irresistible.

- [ ] Would someone happily pay 10-20x this price if it works? (ex: I'd pay $90 to land one $2K client faster.)
- [ ] Does this solve a painful, specific problem that my ideal buyer is actively trying to fix? (get better at content < write a week of posts in 30 minutes)
- [ ] Is this something my buyer would want to use right now (not someday)? (Urgency > importance.)
- [ ] Can someone use this and get a win within 10–30 minutes of opening it? (Fast dopamine hit = high perceived value.)
- [ ] Does it SOLVE without teaching? (It should reduce mental load, not add to it.)
- [ ] Is the transformation obvious? (From: long winded ramble to client-getting elevator pitch in 15 mins)
- [ ] Does the product feel like a shortcut, cheat code, or secret? (Buyers love getting insider tools that others don't have.)
- [ ] Could I confidently say this is something I use or would use myself, and it works? (Is it, like, ACTUALLY helpful?)
- [ ] Does this product naturally set someone up for my signature dish?
- [ ] Could this product make someone say: "If this was $9, I wonder what their main offer is like?"

**BONUS GUT CHECK:**
Would I feel low key nervous about giving this away so cheap because it's that good? (If yes, it's probably perfect 😏)

## Build Your Tripwire Sales Page

Once you have a bomb tripwire, make sure you're making it clear how valuable it is to the person who has just downloaded your freebie using a tripwire sales page. Answer these prompts first, then plug your answers into the outline below. By the end, you'll have a tripwire sales page that practically writes itself (and actually converts).

**Surface level problem:** What are they saying their problem is?
My audience struggles with: _____________________________________________

**But why does it really matter?** Go 5 layers deeper using the 5 Whys:
- Because… >>> Why? ______________________________________________________________________
- >>> Why? ______________________________________________________________________
- >>> Why? ______________________________________________________________________
- >>> Why? ______________________________________________________________________

They feel __________________________ because __________________________ *(Example: They feel stuck because their content gets ignored and they don't know what they're doing wrong.)*

**Hair on fire moment:** When do they finally snap and say "That's it, I CAN'T DO THIS ANYMORE"?
They realize they must fix this when: _____________________________________________ *(Example: They send another pitch and get ghosted... again.)*

**Main Promise:**
This [$____] product helps them _______________________ without _______________________
*(Example: Book their first sales call without running ads)*

**Sexy Product Title:** The __________________________ [template, swipe file, mini-course, script]

**What exactly are they getting? (Deliverables)**
*(Make these sound HAWAIIfied, like: 3 plug and play email scripts, A 9-minute setup video, The 4 item checklist to land your first call)*
