# Promo Copy Planner
*M4.1 · P4: Soft Sales System*

> [!abstract] Quick Read
> **What's here:** A full launch copy planning and drafting workspace covering three email tracks — Push to Register, Push to Show, and Push to Buy — plus matching social post slots, scheduling, and a reusable event/offer blurb.
> **Key insight:** The three-track structure (PTR → PTS → PTB) maps the psychological journey from awareness through attendance to purchase, each requiring a different emotional register. Having the blurb written first unlocks consistency across all 14+ emails.
> **Surfaces in:** Coaching clients through live launch planning; building out Alyssa's own conversion event sequences; teaching the distinction between invite, reminder, and sales email energy.

## Push to Buy Emails

(TO EVERYONE) Now that you've used the conversion event to rapidly warm up your audience you can get them and the rest of your email list excited about your program or about booking a free call with you (aka a sales call, where you will pitch working with you). Use the BOX below to write a little "blurb" that you'll add into ALL your push to buy emails encouraging readers to sign up for your program or book a call with you.

**THE BLURB (aka snippet):**

> As you might know, enrollment is officially open for ____(name of program)____ and I'm taking applications for 2 new clients who want to _______.
>
> If that sounds like you, [apply for a free call here OR go here (your sales page) for more details] >> LINK to book call OR sales page where we will…
>
> [Write sales "bullets" below that will go into every email about what it's like to have a strategy session OR the feature the highlights of your program.]
>
> WHERE TO BOOK/BUY >
> ANY BONUSES OR URGENCY >
> HOW LONG ITS AVAILABLE >
