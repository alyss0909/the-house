## Sales Copy  

# Introducing the Seasonal Promo Planner

The Notion dashboard designed to turn your one signature offer into four completely fresh seasonal promotions without reinventing the wheel. So you can stay offline, keep your marketing low-maintenance, and still book clients during the biggest buying windows of the year.

All you have to do is drop in your offer and follow the seasonal prompts. You’ll walk away with mapped-out campaigns, ready-to-send emails, and a year-end sales plan that actually feels doable.

### What’s Inside

- Map out your sales strategy: Plan your offers, promos, and emails by season, so you’re always aligned with buyer psychology
    
- 25 Email Prompts: Plug-and-play frameworks for warm-ups, nudges, last calls, and more
    
- 13 Reframe Angles: Position your offer for Back to School, Black Friday, the Holidays, and the New Year
    
- 28+ Bonus & Incentive Ideas: Make your promos irresistible without slashing your prices
    
- Swipe Vaults: Save subject lines, CTAs, and seasonal hooks so you never start from scratch.
    
- Performance Tracking: See exactly what worked (and what didn’t) so each year gets easier
    

  

### Why you’ll love it

- Centralized: Everything for your seasonal promos lives in one place
    
- Repeatable: Once you run it once, you can copy it forward for next year
    

Low Maintenance: No big launches, or new content, just your signature dish, plated for each season**
