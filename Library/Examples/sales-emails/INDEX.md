## How to use these

**For voice calibration:** Read the opening hook and the TLDR section. Notice how she frames the product as a system you install once, not a course you take.

**For structure reference:** Alyssa's sales emails lead with a plan or framework first (value-up front), then reveal the offer as the vehicle for implementing it. The CTA appears multiple times, always with a clear reason to click now.

**Rule from Library/Examples/INDEX.md:** Always read at least one real example before writing. Never start cold.
