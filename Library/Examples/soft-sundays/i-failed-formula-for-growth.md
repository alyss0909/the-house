# Here are 3 reallyyyy simple formulas to check that out for yourself:

_These might stress you out, OR they might seem super obvious to you… but regardless I challenge you to make sure you have EACH of these written down to QUANTIFY your actions for next 90 days._

# Formula O1: Profit Projection

💵 Quite literally: How many of your **products do you need to sell,** to hit your sales goal

# Formula O2: Sales Call Conversion

📞 Exactly how many **sales calls you need to book** (based on your show rate and your close rate) to hit your monthly sales goal

# Formula O3: Lead Flow

👯‍♀️ How many **subscribers do you need on your email list** in order to book the required amount of sales calls (and hit your sales goal)
