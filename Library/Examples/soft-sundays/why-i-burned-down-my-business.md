# Why I burned down a perfectly good business (on purpose)

_The weekly productivity report for daydreamers, creative thinkers, ADHDers, and anybody who loves a good check-in. Let's light a candle and set the tone for our soft ceo week ahead._

---

## The Pep Talk

🫶 _the weekly pep talk you didn't know you needed (from your business big sis)_ 🫶

New year / new me / new goals / new vision / new life loading / vibes that are swirling in the air right now — Am i right? Yeah, that's why you're my people.

"And nope, i'm not here to give you a 'wake up call' ☁️ girlie, **keep your head in the clouds** (you have your best ideas up here).

**Instead, I'm here to ask you this:** think about the last time you felt this way when was it? And how long did you stay swept up in it? What pulled you out of it? How long do you usually stay in this creative space before that spark starts to go away?

Is it a few months into the year (when your **to do list is starting to look scary**? 😨), maybe the 1st time you **miss a new milestone**? or maybe you get **distracted by a new goal** or a new project (and abandon the previous one…over and over again)?

Yep, I know the feeling all too well.

And to be honest it was around March of 2024 when I felt the slow dread start to sink in, when that creative magic started to drain from my veins and the regular overwhelm started coursing in instead...

And it was at that moment that I asked myself… **Is this just the life of an entrepreneur?** One minute electric, the next exhausting? Or was there a way to have the **predictability and consistency** of an automated business while maintaining that **creative fuel** that got me into this thing in the first place?

And I won't pretend it was a simple journey, it took a lot of **un-learning, a lot of re-remembering** and a series of dominoes falling that led to completely restructuring my business.

### 🥺 But I'm happy to report that we've landed somewhere incredible..

Over the last 9 months, I've been quietly **restructuring my business** to make more room for this exact thing."

## The House Report

_✍️ Some Inside Intel on what's working, and what's not in various service-biz niches. Reported from BTS of private clients and our students inside Back of House ✍️_

---

**💫 Reason nO1. I wanted my business to be more creative 💫**

"Let me guess, the **entire reason you started your business** was because of a really good creative idea? Yeah, me too.

And what I love about entrepreneurship is the **romanticized idea of a messy office** with too many books, a hot coffee and a good idea. This is what brought most of us here.

🐣 Buuuut, after I came back from maternity leave, I realized just how **little time I actually had** for my big (and small) creative ideas.

**So, I found myself trying to solve my 'need more time' problem just so I could get back to the parts of my business that i loved.**

And since I've never met a good problem i didn't like to solve with automation 🤖 (I mean, I haaad just figured out how to **replace myself for a 9-month-self-funded-mat-leave**), I did what I was good at.

I tried automating this, hiring that person, using this software, thinking those would be the solutions. And I'll be honest, it _worked._ I managed to **get back at least 10 more hours each week**, and I even had more leads coming in.

**But here's the truth:** It ended up leaving me completely overwhelmed. I was spending more time managing people which, let's be real, I don't love. I was also **spending more money**, and my profit margins were shrinking fast 🫠. And it was starting to feel like I'd lost my soul to the business. I **wasn't getting to work on the fun parts** of the business that I actually enjoyed anymore 🤡.

**Because it's not just about automating, it's about automating with intention.**

**🔥 So we burned it all down. (yep, even the profitable parts).**

we made creativity the priority. And we figured out how to have the predictability and consistency without losing the plot.

**So I'm here to REPORT that it IS possible to have a predictable business that**
🧸 Feels **cozy**
👵 Plays the **long game**
💵 Gets **results** (for you and your clients)
💅 And you only have to **set it up once**

With the help of a **new automated email sequence**, & an **updated lead gen strategy**... we got the predictability of an automated business with the rush of a creative business."

---

**🫧 Reason nO2. I wanted my business to be low maintenance. 🫧**

"Here's the thing, when I burned it all down, it wasn't _just_ because I missed the creativity. 🖼 It was because the **entire thing had started to feel overwhelming.**

I realized that somehow, the business I loved had turned into split testing, checking data, and a **never-ending to-do list of things** that didn't actually excite me. The **low-maintenance, cozy business** I built MPQ around? It wasn't my reality anymore. I was literally **outsourcing the stuff that made me light up** while I was stuck with the leftovers 🥴.

### _**I had truly lost the plot.**_

I knew I needed to change two things (🚨 spoiler: it wasn't about getting more leads):
1. 🤓 Spend **less time at my desk.**
2. 😇 Make sure the time I _do_ spend working **feels creative, calm, and aligned,** not chaotic and stressful.

**So, project simplify was born.**

**🐌 We completely restructured the offer suite to prioritize eeeease and sustainability.**

It's designed to be **sold by emails and double prequalify** my community so we know every buyer is the perfect fit.

🥲 **My BABY** (and long-time best seller) **MPQ was closed to make way for something simpler**, clearer, and most importantly, something that gets better results for the people we know best: **creatives who want to spend less time in the admin and more time dreaming up their next genius idea**

🤫 This labour of love is **being unveiled** in just a couple of weeks. 🤫

We also created a **passive membership to support daydreamers** with [a little **something magical** we're keeping secret for now], and I'm literally dying to share it with you, but we'll have to wait till Q2. ⏳

And finally, we **streamlined all our freebies** 🎁 into works of art that **truly support our community** _and_ prequalify our leads. 🖌️

This shift didn't just make my business run smoother, it brought me back to what I _actually_ wanted: **a soft, low-maintenance business 🌸 that works quietly behind the scenes while I spend more time offline, present, and doing the things I love.**"

---

**🕯️ Reason nO3. I wanted more time offline (aka off social media) 🕯️**

"Listen, I'm not gonna act like social media is not a game changer for service providers. Organic marketing has changed the way we run businesses and **I love a doom scroll as much as the next guy 🐇🕳️.**

**But I think we've been sold a lie:** that social media is the only key to growing a business. 🤨

If you're anything like me, and might like to drop off the face of the (instagram) earth for a couple months, 🤢 or the thought of a **hashtag strategy** or **posting daily** makes you nauseous, I have some good news: **it doesn't have to be.**

Don't get me wrong, **social media works wonders for some people**, and there are incredible social strategists out there for service providers.. but it **just doesn't work for me (and my life) 🙈**

I **hate trying to post consistently** (even with the help of a social media manager 🙃), and I hate feeling like it's a task that will **literally NEVER be off my to do list.**

If you're **overstimulated or overwhelmed by social media** too, then you'll be happy to hear where this story leads:

Some time around May **I stopped posting on instagram stories** for a short hiatus (_with every intention to brrrrb! 😆_)
👀 ... But then **i started to enjoy it**
🙈 … And then i started to **dread the thought of coming back**
☝️ That's when I decided to **make social media optional.**
🤓 I poured all my attention into email marketing, evergreen content, and an **automated pinterest strategy.**

🤎 _We prioritized making things feel more human with things like these_ _**soft sunday emails**_ _(hey). We focused on optimizing the entire_ _**business model to convert through email.**_

And although I've been **leveraging email marketing for 10 years**, I took a hard look at everything and asked myself if **I was truly ALL IN** and finally, I can say yes. 💯"

## Time Luxuries

✨ _Life lately: celebrating the little things i'm making time for and what I'm up to while Out of Office (thanks to soft productivity)_ ✨

"❤️‍🔥 If I'm being honest, **2024 felt like tearing down a house I wasn't sure I wanted to live in anymore.** It was raw, uncomfortable, but mesmerizing. **There were days I couldn't tell if I was pruning or burning it all to the ground.**

I paused, I pulled back, I sat with the uneasiness of not knowing what was next, and **somewhere in that quietness, my next steps started whispering back.**

Early last year (March to be exact) I decided the only thing I would do was **tune into what felt good**, not in a fleeting way, but in the soft kinda way that felt like home ☕. I let go of things that looked good but felt hollow, and **leaned into slower, softer, more creatively fulfilling ideas.**

🕯️ And now, 2025 is really feeling like a spark that's ready to catch. **I've spent months tending to what's next: low-maintenance systems**, and pinterest boards full of projects that **make running a business feel human again**.

🤎 But more than anything, I'm blown away by the connections I've made this year, by this **community of soft ceos** who remind me what's possible when we prioritise intention over busyness and **creativity over chaos.**
