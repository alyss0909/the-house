## The House Report

_✍️ Some Inside Intel on what's working, and what's not in various service-biz niches. Reported from BTS of private clients and our students inside Back of House ✍️_

---

**☁️ Strategy O1: Dust off the Trophies**

Repurpose old workshops, client resources, live event recordings into low ticket offers, course bonuses, or sellable recordings.

---

**☁️ Strategy O2: The Second Hand Store**

Repurpose high-performing content (emails, socials, blog posts, podcasts) into new formats. Refresh old recordings with AI (thoughtfully). Pull insights from old launch debriefs.

---

**☁️ Strategy O3: The Low Waste Business**

Upgrade live programs to self-paced/evergreen. Ethical marketing. Lifetime access. Reduce "content pollution" with [pinterest & email](https://preview.convertkit-mail4.com/click/dpheh0hzhm/aHR0cDovL3d3dy5hbHlzc2Fjb2xlbWFuLmNhL2JhY2s=). Ditch fake urgency.

---

**☁️ Strategy O4: Business Hand Me Downs**

License courses/templates to other businesses. White-label DFY content. 1:1 coaching → group offer + self-study downsell. Affiliates.

---

**☁️ Strategy O5: Polish the Heirlooms**

Monthly review of top-performing emails/offers/content. Add one new email to converting sequences. Swap footer offers with evergreen bestsellers.

"Re-commerce isn't just a business strategy. It's a mindset. A quiet form of rebellion against waste, burnout, and constant reinvention. **And it's proof that good ideas deserve to live a little longer.**"
