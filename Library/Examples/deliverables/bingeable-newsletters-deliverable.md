# Bingeable Newsletters — Deliverable

Hey!

I'm excited to hand over my (super) simple "simmer" framework for **turning one boring moment from your week into a scroll-stopping email**, that makes your audience fall in love (and want to buy)

**Finding your repeatable newsletter routine is the difference between a marketing strategy you avoid, and one that becomes the easiest, most natural way you sell every week.**

When you know how to write a bingeable newsletter, you go from having "a list" to a simmering community of ppl who open ALL your emails, understand YOUR philosophy, and really feel like they can trust YOU. **With each newsletter you send, they're inching closer to working with you (or buying right on the spot).**

***This isn't just a warm up strategy, it's a make sales every week strategy.***

The best part is that once you have the framework, **the simmer strategy is already baked in.** Your newsletter takes less than 30 minutes to write and pulls more weight than most of your marketing combined!

**Okay, here's where we begin:**
I want you to think of ONE moment from your week that stands out to you. **I usually like benign moments like the things you might voicenote your bff** (think your kids meltdown over a diagonally cut sandwich or the weird interaction you had with the mailman).

Have that loosely in your mind as you skim though so you can imagine how you'd apply each section to that story!

`There are 5 key parts >>`

`( 01 ) SCROLL STOPPING HOOK`

**🪝 The moment someone opens your email, you're on the clock.**

Your hook is what builds **thumb momentum** inside the email. It either makes them curious, hits an emotion, plants a question in their mind that they want to be answered.

**Regardless, our goal is to keep em scrolling, reading, and CLICKing!**

`( 02 ) SIMMER STORY`

**❤️‍🔥 The easiest way to write regular simmer emails is simple: tell stories.**

A simple story lets your people in, it allows you to subtly teach (not preach) and sneak your philosophy into conversation without even talking about your offer (unless you wanna).

PLUS they're **the most sustainable, low maintenance way to create high converting content week after week, with very little effort from you (even if your life is boring, promise).**

Honestly, the best stories aren't Oscar worthy tales, they're what *just* happened. It's the mundane update you're texting your friend about, the voice memo you're about to delete, a half-overheard convo you chuckled at, or the (weirdly specific) thing your toddler said. It doesn't have to be profound, or really relevant to your niche at all, but you DO have to notice it, **so I HIGHLY recommend starting a story stash spot to pull from when you need it.**

`( 03 ) TAKEAWAY`

**🍿 So What?**

It's time to make your point, and zoom out to make your story MEAN something.

The takeaway should **get them to see from your POV** (hint: it should relate to your bigger business philosophy) and connect your story to why it matters for **them**.

**This is the most important section of your email,** because it establishes you as a thought leader, and an authority they can trust.

Don't worry you don't need a dissertation, think of it like sending a friend a weird little thought **that shifts how they think.**

`( 04 ) VALUE`

**🎀 No, valuable emails aren't "dead" (and who cares if they were) write something worth remembering.**

THIS is what gets people to anticipate, & **actually OPEN your emails consistently**. When you transition to something practical, teachable, or encouraging with no strings attached, it makes your newsletter a high value thing even if they're not buying from you right now. **Get this RIGHT and ppl will start opening every single one of your emails (regardless of the subj line).**

… next you'll see why this matters a.l.o.t.

`( 05 ) OFFER`

**🌶️ THIS IS MY SECRET SAUCE**

If your story naturally tees up your offer, drop an invite. Simply: **here's how I can help if this hit home** (This is how you find out who's ready NOW).

**Right time offers let you sell your offer anytime by sharing it in a natural, relevant way.**

Consider how you can rotate in a fresh reason to share about it, a specific feature to highlight, and a casual way to invite someone in, like: reply "Eat Dirt" and I'll send you the details.

Once you master this, you can tuck your offer into ANY email and find your most ready leads **between** promos.

*🍝 Back of House rotates these like the weekly specials, the dish is the same, you're just describing different notes 🤌*

`( 06 ) SIGN OFF`

**🌹 A cute little sign off is non-negotiable**

Get weird, playful, nostalgic, mama-bear-ish… whatever your style, use this line to give them a lil more of your personality. **Don't overthink it.**

> Ok so now that you know the parts (told ya they were simple, right!?) now it's time to pick ONE moment and bring it through each of the 5 steps. Think of something weird that happened last week, the last voicenote you sent to your bestie, or some little thought that's been on your mind. Don't worry about how it connects yet, just choose! Open a blank doc or head over to the ✏️ writing outline tab on the left of this doc. Jot down one story; then give it a hook, make your point, add one useful takeaway and you're done! `It's time to stop overthinking your newsletter, and start sending`
