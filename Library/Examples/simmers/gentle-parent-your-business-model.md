# let me gentle parent your business model.

_soft productivity, slow marketing, and other musings from one soft ceo to another._

---

K, so I have this problem, [FIRST NAME GOES HERE].

**My daughter HATES getting her hair washed.**

like, she goes full CAT IN WATER MODE.

..and **I'm convinced my neighbours think I'm torturing her 😅** (meanwhile I'm very gingerly rinsing the shampoo out of her hair, with zen flutes playing AND a dry towel over her eyes like we're at a freakin toddler spa).

---

But you knoooow **im a millennial mom, tryna gentle parent** my way through this MOMENT OF RESILIENCE BUILDING. 🥴 Soooo we talk it out, i validate her bodily consent while holding the _-(you gotta freakin bathe babe)-_ boundary..

**And WE GET THROUGH IT** 🥇

It's not easy, but it ISSSSS satisfying! We've got it down, and our HARD WORK PAYS OFF

---

Buuuuuuut as soon as bath time rolls back around it's like **NONE OF THAT EVER HAPPENED** 😑

**We're back at square one.**

And while I desperately hope this is building something in her subconscious (and we're actually getting somewhere?)...it **reminded me of exactly how I used to feel after a launch** 🙈

I'd work my butt off, create the assets, show up, make the content, say the most persuasive things I've ever said…

and the **launch would land, I'd sell spots (!!) I'd breathe again**.

🦗 ...but then…crickets, the leads would stop trickling in within a couple weeks..

Not because the launch flopped.
But because people FORGOT.
And **I had no system to remind them.**

😅 So I'd have to **start all. over. again.** Every month, every quarter, every time I wanted clients. Like our shampoo déjà vu, but with much higher stakes and less juice box bribes.

---

**👀 And i KNOW it's not just me!**
I've seen the most seasoned, wildly smart (AND funny, ANNND hot) **wake up each quarter asking the same question:**
