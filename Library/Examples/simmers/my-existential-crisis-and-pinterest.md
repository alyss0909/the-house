# my existential crisis & pinterest.

whats up [FIRST NAME GOES HERE],

I just finished re-organizing my closet (& NOTHING was sparking joy 🫠).

**Tbh every September, I go through the same existential crisis:**

you see, I waaaant to be a Steve Jobs type with one outfit on repeat, but I keep finding myself at the thrift store.. 🙃

so, my compromise is to create **these little uniforms instead 💅**, summer Alyssa lives in linen pants and a button up, while winter Alyssa goes for (stretchy) trousers and a cozy knit 🤎.

..but EVERY year i find myself in **these awkward in between weeks**, too cold for summer uniform too hot for knits.. and i convince myself

**I need an entirely new wardrobe!**

---

🔮 And as i was taking perfectly good items back out of the "donate" pile **i had a little dejavu to this time last year, when i realised i MIGHT not need to start from scratch.**

That button up I wore all summer **looks brand new layered under a thin sweater**. those linen pants **actually look very coastal grandma with a cute knit**.

**suddenly, she's giving a whole new vibe ✨✨**

---

**😏 And all this pinterest-ing got me thinking about the marketing version of this:**
Your community doesn't wanna KEEP seeing the exact same "outfit" (aka offer), they're bored, ...and cold.

**But that doesn't mean you reinvent your offer.**
👏 i repeat. 👏 NO NEW OFFERS. 👏

[You just show them a new angle, a new season, a new way of seeing it as essential again.](https://preview.convertkit-mail4.com/click/dpheh0hzhm/aHR0cHM6Ly9hbHlzc2Fjb2xlbWFuLmNhL3Byb21vLW5vdGlvbg==)

---

_**Here are two ways to layer (see what i did there) your existing offer this season:**_

**1) 🍎 Back to School:**
Bundle your offer with a "structure" bonus. **Think:** calendars, workflows, checklists, templates, chore charts, any systems that feel like routine.

_(Source: The Promo Planner (Notion Edition))_

**2) 🖤 Black Friday:**
Instead of lowering prices, stack big bonuses. _ex: Grab my copywriting package this week and I'll stack in my Bonus Vault of 95 email prompts, so you're stocked for all of 2026's newsletters._

_**here's how you can do the same:**_
_For [limited time], you'll also get [bonus stack or incentive] so your [desired transformation] pays you back long after [the offer] ends._

_(Source: The Promo Planner (Notion Edition))_
