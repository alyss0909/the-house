**Cite it. Never write it.** The Library holds Alyssa's finished work and anything she has promoted on purpose. Agents read and cite from here; only Alyssa's promotion puts anything new on the shelf. (Alyssa herself edits freely — the read-only rule binds agents, not her.)

## What is NOT here

- AI analysis, voice profiles, frameworks, templates — those are the Analysis desk in the Studio. They are opinion until promoted, even the good ones.
- Anything an agent made and Alyssa hasn't blessed.

## When to come here

- To cite trusted source: her real words, her real curriculum, her real offer facts.
- Hermes loads its tier-1 source from here for every write (SOURCES.md).

## Navigating deep

## The rule

Trust is earned here. Verbatim-Alyssa or explicitly-promoted only. If a file sits in the Library without her words or her stamp, it is misfiled — move it back to the Studio and flag it.
