# Tinna Loaiza - The 5 Patterns

## Suki verdict

Use this as Content OS craft mechanics, not Alyssa source.

It does not contradict Alyssa's carousel system when held at the right layer. It overlaps with what Alyssa already proves in her own top carousels:

- Intimacy maps to Alyssa's field-reporter stance and emotional validation slide. The reader feels caught, not diagnosed.
- Deprivation curiosity maps to Reeve's hook job: create a real open loop or cost-of-inaction without clickbait.
- Tension maps to Cassius's arc job: do not resolve the post too early; let the reader compare two believable paths.
- Visual repetition maps to Charta/Iris template work and Alyssa's own repeated visual systems.
- Narrative maps to the Content OS ladder: hook -> tension -> mic-drop insight -> CTA, with the CTA earned.

## How each rung uses it

**Suki:** Use this when directing source-wiring or arming Mean Alyssa. The question is: which of the five mechanics is weak in this week's machine?

**Angus / Concept:** Check whether the concept creates intimacy and tension before hook-writing begins. A concept that is merely correct but has no felt reader moment or opposing force is weak.

**Reeve / Hook:** Use deprivation curiosity as a hook test. The hook must imply a meaningful open loop, untapped opportunity, or cost of ignoring the post. If it feels obvious, predictable, or hyped, kill it.

**Cassius / Substance:** Use tension and narrative as arc checks. The arc should not give everything away by slide 2, stay vague, or dump the whole room into one post.

**Hermes:** Only after the arc is approved, use the narrative shape as a pacing reminder. Voice still comes from Hermes files and Alyssa originals.

**Charta / Iris:** Use visual repetition when building carousel templates: 3-4 repeatable starting points, recognizable enough that the audience knows whose post it is before reading the name.

**Mean Alyssa gate:** Add one final smell test: which of the five mechanics is missing, and would that make Alyssa say it feels empty, clickbaity, flat, visually forgettable, or like everything got dumped into one post?

## One-line operating card

Every carousel should feel seen, open a non-hype loop, hold tension, look recognizably hers, and move through an earned arc before asking for anything.
