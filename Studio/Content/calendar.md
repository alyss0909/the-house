## How the team executes this
1. Each week the sweep fills that week's lanes with options aimed at the week's offers, using her saves plus wildcards where a lane is thin.
2. She taps the ones she likes.
3. /content-run builds two versions of each tapped post, checks them, delivers in the julia-child format, spreads the month across frameworks so it is not nine How Tos.
4. Her picks get a post date, which fills the board's calendar view. That view is the live version of this grid.

Bump offers (Postmaster, Unignorable) are order bumps on a checkout, not standalone Wednesday CTAs. A Wednesday post CTAs to the main offer; the bump rides along at purchase.
