# Pin Planner — freebie, free

## What this is
A free Notion content calendar (say the thing: an aesthetic Notion dashboard, free) for planning, batching, and scheduling Pinterest pins — with a swipe list of content formulas Pinterest loves, a workflow checklist, Canva pin design templates, and the method for repositioning old posts as evergreen traffic.

## What it sells
Daily traffic from 4 pieces of content, while you do literally anything else. Not a new platform to feed: the slow-marketing exit from the content machine.

## Buying beliefs (what someone must believe before the CTA makes sense)
1. The content machine (constant new posts everywhere) is why they're exhausted — and it isn't the only way to get seen.
2. Content on a search engine keeps working for months or years; content on a feed dies in a day.
3. Their best content already exists; it just needs to be repositioned, not remade.

Best frameworks: Old Way / New Way (content machine vs the slow marketing recipe), Hot Take.
