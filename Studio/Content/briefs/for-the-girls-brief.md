## How to form the live mood (per post, not per file)

1. Read the newest saves and the newest trend brief in `Studio/Content/trend-briefs/` for the cultural tone that is live right now: the season, the pressure, the burnout pattern, the thing everyone is quietly feeling guilty about.
2. Write the mood as ONE plain sentence naming the guilt or pressure and where it came from. Not a concept, not a hook.
3. /saves-sweep puts that sentence on the Notion option row. /content-run loads the row plus this brief. The mood rides the option, not this file.
4. Only when Alyssa picks or edits a mood does it get logged in [[Studio/Content/taste-ledger]]. Only recurring, Alyssa-approved mood patterns earn a line back in this brief.

## Boundaries
