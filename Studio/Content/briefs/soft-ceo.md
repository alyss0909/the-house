# Soft CEO — Sessions (free) + Monthly Notion Template ($37)

## What this is
One sales world, two things. Sessions: a free live monthly planning session with a collective (accountability, office hours, coworking, her productivity planning system). Monthly: a $37 Notion planning template (workbook, weekly action planner, reflection dashboard) sold as the attendee-only special behind the session. The free session is the front door; the template is never pitched cold.

## What it sells
Monthly planning that actually happens — as a cozy ritual with a collective (Sessions), or as a soft system built for scatterbrains that makes sticking to the plan feel like second nature (Monthly).

## Buying beliefs (what someone must believe before the CTA makes sense)
1. Planning the month matters — and left alone, it keeps falling to the bottom of the to-do list.
2. Soft is not unproductive: a collective doing LESS, better, beats grinding solo.
3. Rigid planners fail their brain; a system built for idea people and ADHD brains is the one they'll actually stick to.

## Approved argument lanes (one lane per deck, never mashed)

### Lane A — Planning keeps falling to the bottom of the to-do list (the ritual)
Point: nobody plans their month alone — so make it a standing appointment with a collective: one session, one BIG MONTH GOAL, weekly action plans, and a contingency plan for when life happens. Free.
Villain: solo planning that keeps getting bumped (working IN the biz, never ON it).
Promise: a sacred monthly CEO appointment where the month actually gets planned — with her eyes on your biz.
Best frameworks: How To (her 5-part session flow, taught generic as the WHAT), For The Girls.
Use when: the reader's pain is "I never actually plan" / month drift. CTA = the free Sessions.
Do not mix with: Lane B. This deck sells the ritual and the collective, not the template.
Verbatim source lines: "Does *actually* planning your month keep falling to the bottom of your to-do list? We're putting an end to that." · "Stay inspired all month with a collective of entrepreneurs doing LESS, better." · "📅 Create your BIG MONTH GOAL & a personalised strategy to make it your reality in the next 30 days." · "✅Create weekly action plans that make following through & achieving your goals effortless." · "✨ Design a fool-proof contingency plan (for when life happens) so you'll never feel behind again." · "Did I mention they're free? Join the ☁️ Soft Productivity Collective ☁️ and get in with us"

### Lane B — Soft systems for scatterbrains (the anti-rigid-planner argument)
Point: overwhelming, rigid planners fail creative and ADHD brains — a cozy system that works with your flow is the one you'll actually stick to; gentle guidance and calm focus, not strict timelines.
Villain: overwhelming planners with rigid layouts and strict timelines (and the girlboss energy behind them).
Promise: a system that lets you hit big goals without even thinking about it — sticking to it feels like second nature.
Best frameworks: For The Girls (built for the scatterbrains and dreamers), Old Way / New Way (rigid planner vs soft system).
Use when: the reader's pain is abandoned planners and planner guilt. Deck may name the template; default CTA is still the free Sessions front door.
Do not mix with: Lane A. This deck argues the system design, not the ritual/collective.
Verbatim source lines: "I already know you have big goals, but do you have a system that allows you to hit those goals  without even thinking about it ?" · "Unlike other overwhelming planners, Soft CEO Monthly gently guides you through your month - so that sticking to it feels like second nature." · "Say goodbye to the stress of juggling tasks and hello to a cozy system that lets you flow at your own pace." · "This planner was designed for creative entrepreneurs, idea people, scatterbrains, and those with a soft spot for dreaming big." · "It's more about gentle guidance and calm focus than strict timelines and rigid layouts." · "It's about soft systems that work with your flow." · "Your big ideas deserve a soft place to land"
