# Content desk MAP

This is the map of the content desk. Alyssa's saves become options, her taps become finished posts, and her reactions feed the taste ledger. Volume is the machine's job; taste is hers. The delivery surface is the Notion Content Studio board (data source `collection://89047304-dac5-4668-ac16-a6eaf8721123`, under her Inspo to Idea page). Hosts without slash commands run the same flows via [[Studio/Team Knowledge/SOPs/SOP-041-content-production]].

## The live loop

1. /saves-sweep, daily. Reads her newest Instagram saves (burner-account sync) and turns them into tap-ready options on the Content Studio board in Notion.
2. Her taps. She picks the options she likes; that is her whole job here.
3. /content-run. Approved options become finished posts in the julia-child delivery format.
4. Taste-ledger harvest. Her picks, edits, and results go into [[Studio/Content/taste-ledger]] verbatim.
