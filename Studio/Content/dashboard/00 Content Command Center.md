# Content Command Center

> [!note] The content desk moved (July 16).
> Everything now lives on the **Content Studio board in Notion**, under your Inspo to Idea page. Fresh ideas land there every morning from your Instagram saves, and finished posts wait there for your picks.
>
> [Open the Content Studio board](https://app.notion.com/p/07fc7e627412453f8590d7b867e88613)

## How it works now

1. **Ideas arrive on their own.** Say "/saves-sweep" any time (or let the morning run do it) and 3 to 5 post options built from your latest saves appear on the board.
2. **You tap.** Change a row's Status to Picked if you want it, Killed if you don't. Write anything in Her notes, it all gets read.
3. **Posts come back finished.** Say "/content-run" and every pick becomes complete posts, two versions each, full copy, five hooks, waiting on the board as Ready for Alyssa.
4. **It learns you.** What you pick, edit, and kill is saved as taste, so every week gets closer to your voice.

The rulebook behind the writing is [[Studio/Content/METHOD|The Method]]. Your reactions live in the [[Studio/Content/taste-ledger|taste ledger]].

The old pitch-sheet dashboard, stage pages, and weekly sheets are preserved in [[Archive/content-desk-retired-2026-07-16/dashboard/PIPELINE-CONTRACT|the archive]] if you ever want to look back.
