# Content Outcomes Ledger

The taste ledger learns from **opinion** (her picks, edits, vetoes). This file learns from **outcome** (what the shipped thing actually did). Both feed the loop; neither replaces the other. Nothing here changes future behavior on its own — how an outcome *should* change the next post is a design decision, written up in `Deliverables/2026-07-20_mack_outcome-writeback-design.md`.

## Why a separate file

- The taste ledger is already long and is loaded in full by `/content-run` and `/saves-sweep`. Outcomes are a different KIND of signal (measured, not stated) and want their own refreshable surface.
- Outcomes arrive on a delay (Kit open/click settle ~48h after send; Instagram saves accrue for days). Keeping them out of the taste ledger means a mechanical refresh can rewrite them without touching her verbatim rulings.
- The taste ledger's "Shipped results" section now points here.

### How to read a tag

- **OVER / under / on par** is relative to the median of the SAME bucket in the current pull, within a tunable band (default: open +/-3 points; click +/-40%). The band — and whether "under" should mean anything at all — is Alyssa's call (design doc, Decision 2).
- Open rate is the clean signal on the full list (it barely moves: her broadcasts sit ~40-43%). Click rate on a full broadcast is tiny (~0.1-1%) and noisy; treat it as directional, not a verdict.
- A send with no `Offer / mechanic` tag still gets a baseline tag; it just doesn't roll up to a mechanic yet. Tag it by adding a row to `scripts/data/outcome-map.json`.

## Sales outcomes (STUBBED, gated + attribution-blocked)

Kit exposes a purchases API (`list_purchases` / `get_purchase`) and a products list, so revenue is *technically* reachable — but two blockers stand: (1) in this automation context `list_purchases` is permission-gated (denied on 2026-07-20), and (2) even with the data, attributing a sale to a *specific post or email* is not something Kit hands you — it needs a UTM / coupon / chat-word convention per campaign. See design doc, "Not reachable yet".

## Refresh protocol (mechanical)

Kit is MCP-only in this house (no API key is wired for scripts), so the pull is agent-driven and the math is scripted:

1. **Pull** via the Kit MCP: `get_stats_for_a_list_of_broadcasts` with `status=completed` (paginate as needed). Open/click settle ~48h post-send, so re-pull after that.
2. **Save** the returned `broadcasts` array into `scripts/data/kit-broadcasts-sample.json` (keep the `{"broadcasts":[...]}` wrapper).
3. **Tag** (optional) any new send's offer/lane/mechanic in `scripts/data/outcome-map.json`, sourced from the Products DB "Related Emails" relation.
4. **Run** `python3 scripts/content-outcomes.py`. It rewrites the table above between the OUTCOMES markers with fresh numbers and baseline tags. It never edits anything else and never changes a weight or a rule.

`/content-run` hooks this in at Stage 0 (harvest outcomes alongside her reacts) and records the run's shipped items in the Stage 7 receipt so the next refresh knows what to look up.
