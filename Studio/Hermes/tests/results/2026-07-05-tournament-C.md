**SLIDE 1 — Cover**
> **Old Way, New Way.**

Footer: BACK OF HOUSE

---

**SLIDE 2 — Credibility / open loop**
**Headline:** I've Watched This Pattern In Every Client's Offer.
Body: SAME STORY EVERY TIME. GOOD OFFER, GREAT RESULTS, AND STILL — SOMEONE HAS TO DM ME, WATCH THREE STORIES, OR HOP ON A CALL JUST TO UNDERSTAND WHAT IT ACTUALLY IS. WHICH WAY ARE YOU BUILDING IN?

---

**SLIDE 3 — Agitate**
**Headline:** If It Needs A DM To Explain, It's Not Ready.
Body: YOUR OFFER SHOULD BE SO CLEAR THAT A FEW EMAILS AND A STRONG SALES PAGE DO ALL THE WORK. IF PEOPLE NEED TO WATCH YOUR STORIES OR JUMP ON A CALL JUST TO GET IT.. IT'S NOT READY FOR EMAIL-FIRST.

---

**SLIDE 4 — Value 1 (Old Way defined)**
**Headline:** The Old Way Sells The Thing. Not The Shift.
Body: THE OLD WAY IS FEATURES, DELIVERABLES, A BULLETED LIST OF WHAT'S INCLUDED. IT ASSUMES THE CLIENT ALREADY KNOWS WHAT THEY NEED. MOST OF THE TIME THEY DON'T — THEY JUST KNOW WHAT THEY'RE SICK OF.

---

**SLIDE 5 — Value 2 (Q1 + Q2)**
**Headline:** What They Want vs. What They're Scared It Costs.
Body: WHAT ARE THE AMAZING OUTCOMES SOMEONE GETS AFTER WORKING WITH YOU? NOW — WHAT ARE THEY SCARED THEY'LL HAVE TO DO TO GET THERE? THAT GAP IS WHERE YOUR ENTIRE PITCH LIVES.

---

**SLIDE 6 — Value 3 (Q3 + Q4)**
**Headline:** What Keeps Them Up At 3am?
Body: NOT THE SURFACE PROBLEM. THE THING ALMOST NOBODY KNOWS ABOUT THEM. THEIR INSECURITIES — WHY THEY THINK THEY CAN'T HAVE THE OUTCOME EVEN IF THEY WANT IT. GET DEEP HERE OR DON'T BOTHER.

---

**SLIDE 7 — Value 4 (Re-offer strategy)**
**Headline:** A Good Offer Doesn't Need A New Launch Every Time.
Body: IT SHOULD BE EASY TO RE-OFFER — WITHOUT SLIPPING INTO FULL LAUNCH MODE, AND WITHOUT FEELING REPETITIVE. IF YOU'RE REBUILDING YOUR PITCH FROM SCRATCH EVERY QUARTER, THE OFFER WAS NEVER THE PROBLEM. THE CLARITY WAS.

---

**SLIDE 8 — Aha / content reversal**
**Headline:** They Think They Know The Problem.
Body: SHOW THEM THEY'VE BEEN FIGHTING THE WRONG BATTLE. THAT'S THE WHOLE PITCH. NOT MORE CONVINCING. A DIFFERENT DIAGNOSIS.

---

**SLIDE 9 — CTA**
**Headline:** Comment Yes Chef
Body: AND I'LL SHOW YOU THE AUTOMATED SALES & MARKETING DEPARTMENT FOR CEOS WITH BETTER THINGS TO DO.
