# craft/sales-emails.md

Sales/launch/webinar-invite emails. Faster than Simmers, less diary — more "here's what I've noticed in the market."

## The shape

1. **Hook = observation or provocation**, not personal scene: "You know those people who just… ✨ make things? ✨" / "When people talk to me about their marketing... they often say..."
2. **Contrarian reversal as engine**: "most people **DONT** actually want to go viral... what they really want is to finally stop worrying about WHERE THEIR NEXT CLIENT IS COMING FROM."
3. **The long-setup rhythm still applies**: "So you create shitty posts and half assed emails and graphics you don't love. 🫠 But you're doing it. It's … not your BEST work.. But it's fine because… at least it's done."
4. **Data with personality**, mid-sentence: "Email has been the #1 highest ROI channel for over 20 years." / "batch 1,400 pins in 6 seconds."
5. **"Translation:" device** to compress the framework: "**Translation:** Turn strangers into hungry buyers with a few emails."
6. **CTA repeated 3-5x** (buttons), varied phrasing, escalating: "☕ Save My Seat >>" → "👻 Ghost your Feed Tmrw >>" → "🛎️ Make Your Reservation >>".
7. **Fixed brand close** when pitching BOH ("Meet your business's Back of House" paragraph) + **Pre.S./PS with fresh angle and real scarcity**: "**Pre.S.** We're installing a Back of House System for 10 entrepreneurs..."

## Segment register shift (Kit-verified)

Full-list sends (~8k): brand-forward subjects, 40-51% open. Buyer/registrant segments (60-900): intimacy register wins huge — "you coming?" (76.7%), "you up? free custom bot delivery 🍔" (60%, 32.6% click), "💌 Here, take what worked" (57.8%, 32.8% click). Small list = write like a text to a friend; logistics-plain beats clever ("📌 Class link {NAME}!" = 2.69% click at full list).

## Launch doctrine (from her Lazy Launch module)

A launch = one conversion event + five promo emails. "Your urgency must be real." Reuse 80%+ of launch copy each cycle; rotate the angle, not the system. Deliverable-inside brackets drive clicks: [replay inside], [PLAN INSIDE], [list inside]. Odd numbers beat round ("$12,212 coupon"). Coupon framing casual: "use code ORDERUP... before friday." Never fear-urgency; scarcity of spots and expiring replays only ("replay (& secret menu bonus) comes down at midnight xx").

## Deep-read additions (2026-07-05, from "The silent killer of GOOD work")

Corpus-verified on the full silent-killer BOH email (study: [[Hermes/learning/deep-reads/2026-07-05-1030-silent-killer-of-good-work]]):

- **One coherent emoji family per email, food optional in body.** This email runs a magic-world escalation — 🪄 (x3) on the desire list, 🧚‍♀️ on the rarity beat, 🧙‍♂️ (x3) on the system list — with zero cooking prose until the CTA layer ("🥨 Back of House is that business model. 🥨", "👻 Ghost your Feed Tmrw >>") and the brand-close footer. Framework steps always take the four brand emoji (🐌 BUILD / 💌 AUTOMATE / 📌 GROW / 🧸 SELL).
- **Italic = inner voice, bold = argument spine.** Italics carry the reader's inner monologue and the confession-adjacent lines ("_I could do that_ 🙄" / "_So you create shitty posts and half assed emails..._"); bold stays the skim path. Interleave so the bolded payoff lands last: "Not to make you do more, but to make sure what _you do (once)_ actually **works. 👏**"
- **The diagnosis is a compliment.** Pain pivot elevates the reader: "**It's that you have HIGH STANDARDS.**" Their compromise is a system failure happening to a person with taste — never a discipline failure.
- **Objection preemption in one italic breath**, caps carrying the pivot, product fact in the parenthetical: "_& NO, im not suggesting you never use ai (...) I AM suggesting you ask yourself..._"
- **Dread-subject + instant deflation**: "The silent killer of GOOD work. (pls avoid!⚠️)" — the txt-speak rider is what makes it legal (see ANTI-AI flag). Preview text NAMES the reader ("a call to arms for the creative w high standards"), doesn't summarize.
- **Punctuation register shifts with the email's halves**: trailing dots dominate the confession top third; the system list / STEP block / footer run clean hard periods. List-built sales emails legitimately exceed the ≤~12-period narrative guide (flagged to inbox as nuance).
- **PS is optional when the fixed brand-close footer is present** — this email has none; the brand close takes the re-pitch slot. Opt-out footer stays honest and warm: "(don't worry, you'll still get our regularly scheduled Soft Sunday emails)".
