# Carousel Brief Generator

> Use this to generate a NEW carousel concept from scratch. The pre-production checklist walks you through 5 decisions — each one narrows the next. When all 5 are filled, you have a complete brief ready to hand to Wren for copy execution.
>
> For proof and calibration, use the real carousel folders in [[Library/Examples/Carousels/INDEX|Library/Examples/Carousels/INDEX.md]] plus [[Studio/Analysis/carousel|Studio/Analysis/carousel.md]] and [[Studio/Analysis/carousel-slide-by-slide|carousel-slide-by-slide.md]]. Do not use retired generated brief banks as source material.

---

## Step 1 — Start Here: What's the goal?

Pick ONE primary goal. This determines your archetype.

| Goal | → Archetype | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Saves + non-follower reach | Trend Report (series) | Saves = bookmarked value; series compounds algorithm momentum |
| Shares + brand equity | Manifesto | Permission-slip content gets forwarded to specific friends |
| Maximum cold shares | Any archetype + Visual Joke hook | Screenshot-worthy cover drives sharing before anyone reads |
| New followers from cold | Data Argument | Fear + validation + cliffhanger = highest follow-driver |
| Comments + warmth | Story/POV | Personal narrative invites "me too" responses |
| Direct product conversions | Product Reveal or Step Framework | Teaching = product demo; comment mechanic captures buyers |

## Step 5 — Concept Gut-Check

Before handing to Wren, confirm:

☐ Does this carousel feel like a NEW angle, not a variation of something already posted?
☐ Is the hook genuinely friction-creating for a cold stranger?
☐ Is the teaching point / angle something Alyssa has specific authority on?
☐ Does the archetype match the goal (check Step 1 table)?
☐ Is there a clear prop + color palette that differentiates this from the last carousel?

If all five check — the brief is ready to write from.
