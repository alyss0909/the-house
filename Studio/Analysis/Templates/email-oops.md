## Formula

Hey [first name],

I noticed you didn't [specific thing they missed + the most compelling one thing it would have done for them].

In case you missed it, it's called [product name] and it's normally [full price] but you can still grab it for [discount % or $ off ] [timeframe/last chance framing].

[MOCK UP]  
[CTA BUTTON >>]

[One-liner that makes the product feel stupid-easy / obvious]. So just in case you meant to grab it (or are on the fence), [restate the offer simply incl specific features & benefits].

[Reframe the core problem they have], [bust the belief that's stopping them from buying], [simple solution the product provides].

[CUTE SIGN OFF]
