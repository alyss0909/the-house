# For The Girls

## When to choose this framework
Choose For The Girls when the post is for recognition, relief, and brand trust more than teaching. Identity, permission, self-trust, "I needed someone to say this."

This is its own writing style, not a normal educational carousel with the teaching removed. Before writing, load [[Hermes/craft/for-the-girls-posts]] and read one full example from [[Library/Examples/Carousels/for-the-girls-slide-copy]] in slide order.

Write with inner pressure: the reader needs permission, not a plan. Name the guilt, reclassify the "unproductive" thing as part of the work, repeat short permission lines in one visual world, and include one principle line so the post is more than a mood.

## Content-role cautions
- Freebie: do not attach one. A comment-word ask breaks the permission spell.
- Product or template: no. If the idea needs a product, it is not a For The Girls post.
- Webinar or class: no. Appetite-building belongs in the other frameworks.
- Paid offer: never. No hard sell lives in this lane.
- Waitlist: a soft world-seed is the most this lane carries, and only if it reads as brand, not pitch.
- Affinity: this is the lane. End with recognition, a question, a sign-off, or a soft invitation.

## Offer and CTA cautions
No tactical teaching body, no hard CTA, no forced offer, no normal comment-CTA hook logic. End with a question, sign-off, brand seed, or soft invitation. Do not turn the permission into a pitch.

## Failure modes
- Concept named as a lesson or clever mechanism instead of a simple, felt line.
- Curriculum vocabulary anywhere near the copy ("belief move," "reframe," "the formula"). It kills the intimacy instantly here.
- Re-skin of a published winner: the Jan 22 wasting-time premise with a new noun is a re-skin.
- Quirky-conceit cover instead of a line the reader would send to a friend.
- Framework-specific: sneaking in a teaching framework or steps. Teaching turns permission into homework.
- Framework-specific: pure mood with no principle line. One line has to make the post more than a vibe, or it saves nothing.
