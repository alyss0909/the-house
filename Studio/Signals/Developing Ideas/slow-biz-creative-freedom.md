# Slow Biz as Creative Freedom

## What keeps showing up

This is the oldest signal in the garden — it's been running since 2019 and shows up in virtually every area of the vault. It's the founding thesis of BOH. But it's `becoming` rather than `graduated` because it keeps evolving. The version of this idea in 2026 is meaningfully richer than the version from 2021 or even 2024. The AI context, the "middle ground entrepreneur" positioning, the specificity about what creative freedom actually means (rabbit holes, niche obsessions, going deep) — those are all updates to a thesis that was simpler before.

This signal doesn't need to graduate into a new product. It needs to stay alive as the living soul of everything. It feeds every other developing idea in this garden.
