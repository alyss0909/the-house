# The Idea Garden

This is where ideas live while they're growing. Not tasks. Not archived notes. Growing things.

Every idea here came out of the semantic sweep — it appeared enough times across Alyssa's journal entries, voice memos, and captures that the system named it. From here it develops: it collects new entries that feed it, connects to the Second Brain, gets a thesis draft from Larry, and eventually becomes something real (an offer, a campaign, a framework, a personal essay, a product).

Ideas don't die here. They either become something or they get explicitly dismissed. The system keeps tending them until one or the other happens.

## How this works

- Larry runs the semantic sweep (SOP-013) weekly, monthly, and quarterly
- New clusters seed new idea notes here using `_template.md`
- Each idea note develops over time — new journal entries get wikilinked in, Second Brain connections get added, Larry writes a thesis draft when recurrence hits 6+
- Alyssa reacts to thesis drafts directly in the note — her edits are the intelligence update
- When an idea becomes something, it gets marked `graduated` and linked to whatever it became

The garden gets richer the more Alyssa uses the system. Every voice memo, every saved inspo, every journal entry potentially feeds something growing here.
