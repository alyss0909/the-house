# Signals Index

A living tracker of recurring themes that surface across journal entries, voice memos, and captures. Fed by SOP-013 (semantic sweep). Larry updates this file at every weekly sweep; Alyssa decides on graduation and dismissal.

When a signal hits recurrence 3+ it's `developing`. When it hits 6+ Larry writes a thesis draft in the garden note. When Alyssa edits the thesis draft, it's `becoming`. When it becomes a real thing (offer, campaign, module, essay), it's `graduated`.

**Statuses:**
- `radar` — appeared 1-2 times, watching
- `incubating` — count 3+, pattern is forming, no garden note yet
- `developing` — garden note exists, Larry is tracking and feeding
- `thesis-ready` — recurrence 6+, Larry has written a thesis draft for Alyssa to react to
- `becoming` — Alyssa has edited the thesis draft directly
- `graduated` — became something real (Alyssa confirmed)
- `dismissed` — explicitly decided not to act on
