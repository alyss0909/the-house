# Carousel framework report rebuilt into a writer-usable operating model

## Context

Alyssa came in trying to diagnose why the content team keeps producing social posts she does not believe in even though the house has a huge amount of carousel analysis, examples, offer knowledge, and craft references. The first answer drifted toward more categories, which repeated the root problem: too many labels, not enough usable writing logic.

The session became a deep rebuild of the forward-use carousel report. The goal changed from describing what Alyssa's past carousels did to giving Hermes/Suki/a future writer a model they can use to write a new persuasive carousel from an offer, a buying belief, and a content idea.

## What I shipped

- Rebuilt [[Studio/Analysis/carousel-winning-flow-report]] around one simple carousel spine: cover hook -> second hook -> old way/problem -> proof or validation -> teaching -> turn -> CTA.
- Collapsed the over-categorized framework draft into five memorable frameworks Alyssa can actually refer to: House Report, Old Way / New Way, How To, Hot Take, and For The Girls.
- Preserved the older drafts Alyssa liked as history instead of deleting them: [[Studio/Analysis/carousel-winning-flow-report-draft-2026-07-17]], [[Studio/Analysis/carousel-winning-flow-report-draft-2026-07-17-layered]], [[Studio/Analysis/carousel-winning-flow-report-draft-2026-07-17-pressure-framework]], and [[Studio/Analysis/carousel-winning-flow-report-draft-2026-07-17-five-frameworks-pre-deep-pass]].
- Added Solution 4 to [[Studio/Analysis/carousel-winning-flow-report]]: the shared spine is the default operating order, not a rigid outline. Product Plan, For The Girls, Hot Take, and House Report can bend the spine for specific reasons.
- Added Solution 5 to [[Studio/Analysis/carousel-winning-flow-report]]: every brief now names content role, so CTA strength and product timing are decided before writing.

## Decisions made

- **Question:** What should a carousel brief name before writing starts?
  **Decision:** `offer + buying belief + framework + content role + hook`. Hook comes last, and content role controls product timing/CTA force. Durable home: [[Studio/Analysis/carousel-winning-flow-report]].
- **Question:** Should market pressure and inner pressure be standalone tags or briefing layers?
  **Decision:** No. Pressure is writing logic for slides 2 to 4, not another mandatory taxonomy layer. Durable home: [[Studio/Analysis/carousel-winning-flow-report]].
- **Question:** Should the report keep the eight-ish old named flows?
  **Decision:** No for forward writing. Keep their useful copy steps folded into five frameworks and preserve old drafts as history. Durable home: [[Studio/Analysis/carousel-winning-flow-report]].
- **Question:** Should we edit [[Studio/Analysis/carousel-slide-by-slide]] now?
  **Decision:** Not yet. Alyssa wants the framework names/model settled first before that favorite source doc gets tagged. Durable home: [[Studio/Analysis/carousel-framework-source-loading-plan]].

## Insights

- The deeper content failure is not lack of knowledge. The house has examples, slide-by-slide analysis, captions, offer maps, external craft notes, and old frameworks. The failure is that agents get asked to reconcile too many layers at once, then summarize instead of writing. Durable home: [[Studio/Analysis/carousel-framework-source-loading-plan]].
- The best forward model is not "what framework is this whole post?" It is "what simple persuasive path makes this offer belief feel true?" The framework should answer that fast, then the shared spine carries the slide jobs. Durable home: [[Studio/Analysis/carousel-winning-flow-report]].
- The useful parts of the old two master arcs still survived, but as pressure logic inside frameworks: market pressure for outside-world shifts, inner pressure for self-blame/relief/permission. Durable home: [[Studio/Analysis/carousel-winning-flow-report]].
- This is a second-appearance lesson. Earlier logs already said real carousel examples outrank generated frameworks; today clarified the next step: a source-grounded framework still fails if Alyssa herself cannot use it to write. Durable home: [[Studio/Team Knowledge/PRECEDENT-INDEX]].
