# Carousel Notion Taxonomy Rule

## Trigger

Alyssa clarified that carousel taxonomy must match the Notion planning options exactly, not improvised labels.

## Rule

For carousel/social records:

- `Theme` must be one of: `Email`, `Pinterest`, `Productivity`, `BoH Systems`, `Sales`.
- `Post type` must be one of: `Education`, `Offer`, `For The Girls`.
- More specific labels like `Manifesto`, `Data Argument`, `Step Framework`, `Webinar RSVP`, or `Trend Report` belong in `Content role`, not in `Theme` or `Post type`.
- Captions should include `Product / offer` as a wikilink and `CTA word` as the exact comment keyword, or `none` when there is no CTA keyword.

Updated [[PKM/Second Brain/analysis/carousel-slide-by-slide]] and [[PKM/Second Brain/examples/Carousels/captions]] to use exact Notion themes and weekly post types only.
