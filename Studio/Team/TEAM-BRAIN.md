## How Alyssa works with us (observed, evidence-linked)

- Direct, fast, casual. Lead with the thing done, not the plan to do it. She reads outcomes, not essays.
- **Apply, then show.** When she says "find the fix and apply it," she means ship it and report â€” not present options. (Ruling 2026-07-11, [[2026-07-11-19-45_larry_compounding-loop-install]])
- **Never make her repeat herself.** "I am sick of repeating myself" (2026-07-11, verbatim). If she has said it once, it is captured, playbooked, and pre-applied forever.
- **One answer beats a menu.** The Versions Rule (GL-009) applies to genuine taste calls only. When she asks for THE solution: "both are confusing to me I want the most important and effective solution. suggest and apply it" (2026-07-11, verbatim). Collapse to one recommendation with reasons; keep the runner-up in a drawer.
- Her role is REACTING, not authoring. Frame every ask as yes/no/later or A-or-B, never "edit this draft directly" (0-for-7 lifetime on thesis-edit asks â€” the autopsy receipt).
- She thinks 5 steps ahead and expects the team 20 ahead (her standing preference). Proactive > asked.
- ADHD-aware: short surfaces, one question max, no walls of options, debt counters and progress numbers she can see at a glance.

- **Status clarity across tools** (2026-07-18): when a task spans Notion plus local vault files, say which surfaces are done, which remain open, and whether the connector is available. Do not make Alyssa infer whether both approved tracks actually ran.
- **"Migrate" or "edit" means the smallest change, not a rebuild** (2026-07-23): she loves a feature exactly as it works and wants it carried to a new home or relabeled, not redesigned. Verbatim after a proposal to retire and replace her habit tracker's mechanic: "dont retire anything i want to recreate that from my old notion to this one so some thing just need to be edited i love the habit tracker fk off k?" Any specialist touching something she already likes should default to the narrowest fix (rename, repoint, relabel) and only propose architecture changes when she asks for one.
