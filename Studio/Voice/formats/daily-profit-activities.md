# Daily Profit Activity Descriptions

Alyssa's own template for writing each activity inside the **Daily Profit Activities / Profit Menu** Notion product (see also the layout inspo: 🧠 The Dopamine Biz Boost). This is the description structure every profit activity card should follow.

Related: [[Studio/Voice/formats/README]] · voice guardrails in [[Studio/Voice/KILL-LIST]].

## The structure

Each task includes the following sections — even if one is super short. Just enough context to inspire action *and* show how it contributes to daily offers, without over-explaining.

### 1. Name + One-Liner Hook (bolded)

A high-level framing of what this tactic does, why it's clever, or how it helps sell softly. *Vibe meets value.*

> *Example (Humble Brag):*
> **"The Humble Brag" is my favourite way to make an offer.**

### 2. Why It Works

1–2 sentences on the psychology or strategic reason behind it. Why is this worth doing? What's the business case behind the fun?

Tone: *"Here's the thing…"*, *"This works because…"*, *"The real win here is…"*

> *Example (Blog Post):*
> **Your blog post will solve one micro problem for anyone entering your community AND help to separate your ideal clients (those who will resonate with the subject) from those who aren't a great fit.**

### 3. The How-To / What To Say

A short script, structure, or specific options someone could plug into an email or content. Show them how to execute the idea today — ideally in 5–15 minutes or less. Use a mini list or walk through a sample line.

> *Example (Blog Post):*
> Write blog posts like:
> - The best website platforms for beginners
> - How to choose between Squarespace and Wordpress
> - Which theme is best to choose with Squarespace

### 4. Tie-In to Offer (soft CTA optional)

Close with how to connect this activity to a real offer — a line to add, a poll, a CTA, or a tag/segmenting suggestion. Not salesy, just a reminder that this is an *income-generating* task.

> *Example (Humble Brag):*
> see how this gives value, brags about the results your clients are getting AND invites them on a discovery call with you? WIN WIN WIN.

## Optional add-ons

- 🪄 **Emojis** to set tone and make it skimmable.
- 💡 **Italic notes** to hint at variations ("you could also…").
- 🧂 **Sprinkle in your own results,** little stories, or client examples when possible — it makes it sticky.
