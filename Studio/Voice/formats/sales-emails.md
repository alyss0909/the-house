## Launch doctrine

From her Lazy Launch module, cited in [[Hermes/craft/sales-emails]]: **a launch is one conversion event plus five promo emails.** "Your urgency must be real." Reuse 80%+ of the launch copy each cycle; rotate the angle, not the system. Deliverable-inside brackets drive clicks ([replay inside], [PLAN INSIDE], [list inside]). Odd numbers beat round ("$12,212 coupon"). Coupon framing stays casual ("use code ORDERUP... before friday"). Never fear-urgency вАФ scarcity of real spots and expiring replays only.
